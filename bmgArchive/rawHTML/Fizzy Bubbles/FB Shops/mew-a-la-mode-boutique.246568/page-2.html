Alright, I'd like to pay 100 to recolor Willow the Shiftry so that her leaf fans are a lighter shade of green to match a Leavanny's leaves, her brown body is a darker shade of brown to match the sides of a Timburr's log, and her eyes are light red, same as a Leavanny's eyes. As for the reason... I basically gotta give you Willow's life story.

Willow was a Leavanny who lived in a forest in Unova, somewhere in the general vicinity of between Floccesy Town and Virbank City. She looked after young Pokémon that lost their parents one way or another, made leaf clothes for them to protect them from the cold, cheered them up when they were sad, things of that nature. One day, a Houndoom attacked, and wound up setting the forest on fire. Willow successfully evacuated the young Pokémon, though in doing so, lost her life.

After dying, Willow's spirit wandered, still wanting to help others, and eventually, she came across a depressed Timburr in the old Adoption Center, who was seriously considering suicide, convinced nobody would want to adopt her. Willow took up residence in the Timburr's log, talked her out of ending her life, and the two got to talking some more, eventually coming to see each other as sisters. That Timburr is Millicent, my Timburr. Willow has stayed within Millicent's log ever since.

Until recently. After GS's Shiftry perished in the battle against Stewie on Stargazer Island, the body was left at the Adoption Center where it would wait for a new soul to take up residence within it. Willow caught wind of this, and saw it as an opportunity to be an actual Pokémon once more, and convinced Millicent to take her there. After some discussion, Millicent realized this was what was best for Willow, and encouraged her to go for it. And once Willow's soul inhabited the Shiftry body, it underwent a few physical changes to reflect its inhabitant- leaves and eyes recolored to reflect her Leavanny past, wooden body recolored to reflect the log that had been her home for so long.
