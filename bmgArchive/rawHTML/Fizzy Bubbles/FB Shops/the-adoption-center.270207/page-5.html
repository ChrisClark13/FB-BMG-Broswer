Cy wandered somewhat aimlessly, but he knew where his feet were taking him. The Adoption Center was nice to stroll around, but only one of its habitats had Pokemon he wanted. So he found himself in front of the Bug area once more, to find it thriving. A few Pokemon were in here this time. He briefly considered a sour-looking Durant before noticing a Dwebble perched on a rock. It had a friendly look on its face, and didn't seem to mind when the rock it sat on produced claws and tried to shoo it off. Wurmple hopped off Cy's shoulder to introduce himself.
Hello. I am Wurmple. You are?
Uhh.... Hi! I'm Clay. Are you with that human?
Yes. He is my Trainer, a sort of parent and friend.
Neat. Do you have food?
Er... Yes, we have food.
Cool! Can I have some food?
If my Trainer likes you, he may want you to come with us-
I just want the food.
Erm... There will be food, yes.
Awesome!
The Dwebble bounced happily off the rock and grinned at Cy when the Trainer knelt down in front of him. "What do you think, Wurmple? Do you like this one?"
I think he's a complete featherbrain. Harmless enough, I suppose.
Cy held out his hand for the little Bug Pokemon. Dwebble stared at him, not comprehending. Cy laughed. "If you want, you can come with us," He said. "Sound good to you?"
Dwebble looked up at him and bobbed his head. As long as there's food!

TL;DR: Adopting this Male Dwebble. Here's the $200. Putting him in a Luxury Ball, ,naming him Clay, declaring his ability to be Sturdy.
