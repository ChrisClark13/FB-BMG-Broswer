Pokébank
Is your mom spending your savings without asking? Or are you simply looking for a place to store your cash without worries?

Here at the Pokébank, we want all your money to stay safe. Our staff assures the highest quality of service not only in serving you, but in managing your savings. Not only that, but this the only bank that rewards trainers for their efforts to explore the land! So not only are your savings safe with us, with effort it will also grow.

So what are you waiting for? Open up an account with us today, for a stress-free banking experience.​

Rules:
  1. All cash you own must be recorded in the bank.
  2. You may make only one post in which to edit new transactions. Please keep deposits and withdrawals under separate subheadings.
  3. Once the shop has been updated with your new balance, you must make a new post for your transactions. Please do NOT edit your old post thereafter.
  4. You will receive $250 per zone reply of 250+ words. Should you post less than the 250 word minimum required of a zone reply you will receive no cash at all. Please include a word count for all zone reply claims.
  5. All deposits and withdrawals must have an accompanying link to where it was earned/spent.
  6. If there was a transaction you forgot to add or if you expect to be away for a period of time, contact the Shop Owner to make arrangements.
  7. For cash obtained through the Staff Rewards thread, please specify at what FC step you obtained the cash from and what date this was achieved.
