Welcome to Fizzy Bubbles!​

Welcome to the wonderful world of Fizzy Bubbles! We would be very pleased if you would join our humble RP. In order to join, the process is very simple! To join, simply sign out the form below.

Code: 
Trainer Name:
Starter Pokemon:

After you have filled out the form, we will send you a brief registration test, making sure that you can RP well enough to keep your head above the water, so to speak. If you pass, you will be able to join Fizzy Bubbles, making a Member Post and picking up the following Starter Pack for all adventurers!

Fizzy Bubbles Starter Pack:
x1 Backpack
x1 Pokedex
x1 Mega Ring
x1 Z-Ring
x1 Dynamax Band
x1 Fishing Rod
x1 Berry Bag
x1 Pokeblock Case
x5 Pokeballs
x5 Rare Candies
x1 TM Return or Frustration
$3000 Pokedollars

Starter Pokemon Rules: While the starter rules in FB were more strict in the past, currently they will allow you to pick up any non-evolved Pokemon in the game. The only exception to this is Pikachu, because its the mascot of Pokemon and a fan favorite. Starters start at Level 5, but you can choose other things like abilities (including HAs) at will. You can also add one Egg move or MT move of your choice. There are only a few Pokemon which are considered so rare that they can't be starter Pokemon:

-Legendaries (including Ultra Beasts), Fossil Pokemon, Type: Null, Phione.

Starters cannot be Shiny.

Registration is OPEN!

Trainer Name Moon Brandt (common usage: Brandt)
Starter Pokemon Zorua

Background
Brandt lost his parents in a mysterious accident when he was younger. Because he was in a coma for a couple of weeks, he is unable to remember what he experienced, but in the other hand, what happened in the accident remains as a secret ever since. After that incident that occured when he was at a very young age, his uncle from another region, Peter, brought him to his home. As Brandt started a brand new chapter in his life, he began to live with a brand new family as well. His childhoold friend and cousin Cheren became his best friend after that.

During those years, Brandt followed Cheren's steps and focused on his education just like Cheren. Cheren's ultimate goal was becoming a gym leader, but Brandt could never fill the will inside to become anything, therefore, he kept searching for his options. One of those years, Brandt found a hurt Zorua in the woods and cured him by the help of Cheren. When Zorua felt alive again, he stuck to the house, rather than leaving the family behind. Along with Cheren, Brandt became best friends with Zorua as well.

After the high school, Cheren took his time to go to the college in another city and study more to become a worthy gym leader. In the meantime, Brandt only worked at supermarket as cashier to meet his needs until he finds out what he desires to be. He moved in another house and took only Zorua with him. Years later, just a couple of weeks before Cheren returned home for good, Brandt found out that his house was searched, but it was an unusual attempt of robbery. The house was left so tidy that it was almost impossible to realize that someone broke in.

When Cheren eventually returned from college, Brandt told him everything and Cheren responded with an invitation to his journey. Cheren stated that he was already going to set off across the region to discover more about Pokémon and understand their nature, and he wanted Brandt to go along with him to find the hints and experience more about life. Accepting the offer, Brandt and Zorua made their last preparations, and left their hometown for once and all to fullfil their dreams and discover more.

Trainer Name: Wendelle (Nickname: Wendy) Age: 75 – Height: 4' 11" – Weight: 98 lbs.

Starter Pokémon: "Cyril" ~ Grimer (Regular) ~ Naughty, Scatters Things Often ~ Level 5 ~ Ability: Sticky Hold ~ Moves (IV): Pound, Poison Gas, & Harden

Background: Wendelle is an elderly woman. Only her wheelchair grants her motility, although her slow, shaky hands can scarcely propel it. Cyril, her Grimer, often pushes her to her destinations, although the playfully spiteful Pokémon tends to make silly paths to these places or deceptively normal paths to any other location. Naughty Cyril, who makes a mess of her home for his pleasure, maintains joy in Wendelle. She never married, and she spent the majority of her adulthood in solitude. Cyril has lived with the woman for only six years, and his presence exponentially decreased the probability of the appearance of another guest (if they can smell).

Wendelle has lived in a small, shabby yet cozy house in a thin forest that cradles a pond. The creek which gently spills into the little pool became brown & sludgy throughout a period of a few months when Wendelle had approximately sixty-seven years. Cyril the Grimer emerged some years after the pond had become stagnant & black like the underbellies of clouds in the night, and he lived around the pond and travelled up the creek to find the tasty sources of his birthplace. Wendelle avoided the filthy water & watched natural rainfall maintain her garden of berries & vegetables, which was her only source of sustenance; in a wheelchair & muscularly frail, she could not physically endure a trek to even a nearby town. Bug & Bird Pokémon of the forest occasionally stole from her garden, but she could still maintain herself & have enough food to keep for the season of winter.

The Grimer was tarnishing the forest. Wendelle could wander through the flatter parts of the woods, and she had begun to see trails of bedraggled, dead grass & other plants. Although most of her senses were almost useless, her bulbous nose detected a slightly less acceptable scent among the trees. She remembered how Grimer had made ruins of the local park by childhood home, so she began to believe that a similar culprit roamed the woods. She saw less Pokémon in her garden & in the trees & anywhere.

Eventually the Grimer found Wendelle's home, and he destroyed the garden of fragile crops. When she awoke in the morn, Wendelle discovered this putrid carnage upon her vegetation, and the bits of purple glop which festered around the brown roots & wrinkly fruits that had twisted & died provided the woman fuel for hatred. Wendelle had held latent ferocity since she was in adolescence, and one need only aggress against something that was precious to her, e.g. her food. Revenge surged in the old woman's mind with terribly pungent conviction, and she rolled through the traversable sections of the forest until her arms, blurry & gray in the moonlight that had poured into her world, failed. Anger throughout an entire day & an explosion of frustration spurred all of her emotions from her past & her present, quiet heart, and the woman cried bitterly. The emotional & physical fatigue sadly slipped Wendelle into slumber.

Wendelle awoke with sensations of satisfactory rest & ugly grogginess. The Grimer slept at her wide, bare feet which daintily hung over dead grass. Of course, Wendelle thought when she comprehended her situation. You should feel guilty, although Wendelle, who had not spent much time with any Pokémon, was uncertain of whether the Grimer could feel guilt. Anyway, she had not the energy to feel hatred; the serenity of the morn soothed her to be patient. She waited, among wiry trees and brown, gunky ground. When the Grimer stirred, it saw the open, watery eyes of Wendelle, and the purple glop visibly deflated and he allocated the gaze of his large eyes. The old woman had not seen shame in several years.

It was almost unnecessary for the Grimer to express his apology, and he failed to understand this for a long time. Wendelle also failed to understand this, but she had already & spontaneously forgiven him. She simply said, "I need food," and the Grimer arose to begin pushing her wheelchair to, she hoped (and received), a source without pollution. He was, at least, faster than the old woman's hands. So began the relationship between the old woman and the Grimer, whose body reminded him of the milky slop that was the result of breakfast cereal she ate when she was a child. She found the name "Cyril" easier to pronounce.
