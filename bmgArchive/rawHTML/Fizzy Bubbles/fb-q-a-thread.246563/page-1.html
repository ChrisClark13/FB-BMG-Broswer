Hi, I have two simple questions:
  1. Firstly, I know that I have to keep a record of our Pokemon's movepools, however, I was wondering how I deal with Pokemon whose movepools vary across games. For example, Alolan Sandshrew knows Bide at level 3 in the gen 7 games, however, it knows Mist instead at level 3 in SwSh. Do I combine all movepools, or do I use only one (and in that case, which)?
  2. Secondly, I was wondering what the starting bond is for starters? I figure it's zero but I just want to make sure in case starters have a higher bond than normally obtained Pokemon.
Thanks in advance.

1. The rule of thumb is that Pokemon have access to their movepools from all main franchise versions (LGPE included) and you take whatever generation an attack is learned lowest. The timing couldn't be more perfect for you to ask this because one of our members literally just coded a website that lists the level up dex for all Pokemon with the lowest level movesets for each species, found here: Pokemon Move Set Viewer

2. As you suspect, all Pokemon start with 0 Bond, unless you capture them in a Luxury Ball, which would start them at 10.
