.:Trainer Profile:.

Name: Ethan Rivers
Age: 23
Birthday: February 6th
Hometown: White Forest
Appearance: Standing at 5'10 and weighing approximately 210 lbs with dark black wavy hair, blue eyes and tanned skin. Ethan is fairly muscular due to all the exercise regiments his training has put him through. He tends to wear rather comfortable clothing in case he needs to dive into action at any given time, and plans his attire accordingly to where he is traveling to. Usually, dressing in a dark shirt, ripped faded out jeans combo, with running shoes incase he ever needs to jog a long distance.

Personality: Personality wise, Ethan is a very strong headed and stubborn person when it comes to what is morally right or wrong. He over thinks things, and is very detail originated, scanning any given room he has entered in a matter of seconds.

In his mind he has gone through every possible scenario before they even happen, having come up with a game plan before an opponent can even think of countering him. He is a child, and likes to have fun but not in the expense of others. He has a soft spot for those that truly work hard at what they do, as well as for children and Pokémon that for one reason or another the world has casted away to fend for themselves.

Though he doesn't have any close friends, preferring the company of Pokémon instead, he has but only one person he cares about deeply enough, that if anything were to happen to her, not even a natural disaster would stop him from trying to reach his girlfriend if she were in danger.

Background: Ethan came from modest beginnings, his father was a teacher while his mother was a stay at home wife. They didn't have much but what they had was enough to get by, they never believed in material things and preferred it that way.

When Ethan was young, he wanted to travel the world but his parents didn't believe their young son was ready to face the vast world before them, so they held him back, which caused their child to act out and run away. The day Ethan ran away, his home engulfed into flames with his parents perishing inside. He never understood why, how, or even who would do such a heinous thing as to take the only family he knew away from him. After not finding any clues to who might of been the culprit, the authorities were convinced Ethan must've done it- since no one believed him, he chose to flee which only made the situation far worse.

As a young run away, no one paid Ethan any mind. He was quick and sneaky, stealing what he and his Pokémon needed to get by. As years progressed he learned the craft and became a notorious thief under the name 'The Phantom'; his disguise: a mask and a purple cape over a black tux. For the longest time he searched for something to fill his empty heart, but no heist big enough would ever do, it wasn't until he met a young med school student, that saved him from an almost fatal wound, that he found a reason to live.

Unfortunately, the blood he spilled that night, linked to his real identity and thus fleeing from the clutches of justice once again to another region where he would start a new. That's where his story truly begins...
