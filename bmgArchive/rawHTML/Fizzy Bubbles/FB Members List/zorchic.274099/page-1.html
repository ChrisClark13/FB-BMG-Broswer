    Userbar by @Felly​
    Name: Jake Celeste
    Age: 14
    Gender: Male
    Birthday: April 2
    (Wishlist on Bulbagarden, Wishlist on UPN [Ultimate Pokémon Network], Secret Base, Visionary Glade Stories, List of Owned Types, Affinity Avenue Breeding "Log")

    Custom Sprites:


    Appearance: Jake Celeste stands at 5’4”. He has light brown hair, hazel eyes, and slightly tanned skin. He wears a plain blue shirt most of the time, and he also wears black gloves with green stripes on the fingers. Jake normally wears cargo shorts the color of a pizza crust, and casual black and blue sneakers/tennis shoes (if tennis even exists in the Pokémon world). He wears a black and green baseball cap (similar pattern to Ash’s hat from the Advanced Generation series in the Pokémon anime). Only, in the beginning of his journey in Fizzytopia, he has decided to ditch the hat for now until he becomes a more "wild" adventurer. Jake put his hat in his adventurer's backpack (bag), to be stored until he decides to wear it.

    Background: Jake’s past is mostly a secret. The one thing people know about him is that he has the gift of aura manipulation, i.e. he can create barriers, aura spheres, and aura bubbles, and he can transmit aura energy to others in emergencies. (Transmitting aura to others is potentially fatal, so he avoids doing this.) He can read the auras, and thus thoughts, of all living things. This is because a living thing's aura changes based on what they're thinking. Jake's strong aura also protects him from being affected by mental assaults such as Confuse Ray or Hypnosis. His family line through his father inherits this gifted trait. Jake’s Buizel was given to him as a gift from his father, who taught the Sea Weasel Pokémon a move it could not learn otherwise (Ice Punch). Jake’s origins are in Pastoria City, Sinnoh.

    Jake is capable of speaking Skarmory language ever since his family programmed the autopilot in their helicopter, the Skarmorix. A quirk in the machine's code caused it to speak only Skarmory language when communicating, but the Trainer's family adapted, using the feature to help them study Pokémon language(s). However, Jake realized that he'd only be able to communicate with the Skarmorix's autopilot if he learned Skarmory language, since machines don't radiate auras, so he asked his Lucario to teach him, and the aura-using Pokémon happily agreed. Since then, the training Aura Guardian has been able to speak the language of the Armor Bird Pokémon, Skarmory.

    Personality/Battle Style: Jake is normally calm and collected, and is willing to help out a friend in need. He doesn't usually get angry, normally using snarky remarks and replies on his side of an argument, but when he does get angry, he explodes. Like a raging Dragonite. So don't anger him, case closed.
    Jake is clever and witty in battle, using tricks to lure his opponent into a favorable position for him. He prefers speed and power over defense, and trains his Pokémon likewise. He always seems to know what an opponent is planning, and is an expert at thwarting such plans in unorthodox ways. He is familiar with the spinning technique often used to deflect special attacks, and, while he uses that technique to its fullest, he also exploits his opponents' attempts to use it. It's all science; if the target spins clockwise, Jake’s Pokémon aims their special attack slightly to the opponent’s right. If the target spins counterclockwise, then Jake’s Pokémon aims slightly to the opponent’s left. The target’s spinning generates suction, making special attacks surround them like a Fire Spin instead of bouncing off.
