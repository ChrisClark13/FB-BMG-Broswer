Age: 25

Trainer Info:
Stark began his life as a trainer later than most people, having been undecided with what he wanted to do with his life until he was nearly 20 years old. He has found a natural affinity toward Fire and Flying pokemon in particular and while looking for a balanced team he has found himself filling his team with an abundance of these two types.
He tends to normally wear a long sleeve black shirt with black jeans with a brown belt and black biker boots under a long red coat. However, he also carries a long sleeved green shirt, a white T-shirt, blue jeans, brown hiking boots, and a brown leather jacket in his pack for different environments and change of clothing. His blond hair runs to about the middle of his back and is generally pulled back into a ponytail with a few of the bangs generally hanging down in his face or off to the sides depending on when the last time he pulled his hair back was.

Mediville
Stark began his journey in the countryside surrounding Mediville with Cyndaquil by his side. It was here that he met a young girl named Bella who had found herself chasing her Hoppip as it was caught up in a gust of wind. Normally this wouldn't have been a problem, but the helpless Hoppip soon found itself floating into a Spearow's territory. Seeing the scene unfold before him, Stark took the initiative and sent Cyndaquil into his first battle with the Spearow. After a hard fought battle, Cyndaquil managed to defeat Spearow with a combination of guile and physical power. During the fight Stark found himself admiring the the little bird's determination which led to his decision to capture the Spearow.
