Fizzy Fright Fest​

● CARNIVAL RULES:
- You may only participate in one activity at a time and you may only partake in an activity once each.
- As you participate in each activity, it is encouraged that you enjoy it with the company of your Pokemon. A maximum of 1 Pokemon may assist you with each activity and they may use up to a maximum of 3 attacks.
- Based on how much you and your Pokemon are able to generate excitement at each activity, you will be scored and given Carnival Coupons to cash in for prizes at the end of the event!
- You have until the end of the art contest to participate in all 6 attractions!
- List of Attractions:
- Hall of Mirrors: A mind-bending maze of optical illusions! Focus the mind and plan out a course that will lead you through the menagerie of mirrors the fastest!​

As the ticket booth spit out its fifth ticket, Melissa's three Pokémon burst into celebration, congratulating Eve for her sharp shooting. Melissa pocketed the tickets and then scooped up her Eevee, giving her a tight hug and praise. "Nice job, Eve!"

"Vee eevee," Eve replied, ducking her head. "Vee eevee eevee."

"Still." She set Eve back down and then led the way towards the Hall of Mirrors. "It looks impressive. And it's not like you didn't try."

"Vee," Eve admitted.

The small group wound their way over to the Hall of Mirrors, chattering quietly to try to fend off the atmosphere. Melissa appreciated her team's attempt to set her at ease, but, as they came up to the next attraction, she figured it was probably a better idea to bring just about everyone else back; she didn't need half of the group to get lost in the maze when they took a left when they should've taken a right.

They slowed to a stop before the Hall of Mirrors. "Alright," Melissa sighed, sizing the place up. "Cirrus, Eve, you guys should probably return."

Eve nodded and Cirrus shrugged, but they both returned themselves. Melissa then plucked her third Pokéball off her belt and sent out her Sableye. Ore materialized with a chitter, glancing around curiously at their surroundings.

"It's a maze of illusions," Melissa said, nodding at the entrance to the Hall of Mirrors. "So, I'm thinking Foresight, Detect and then Nasty Plot."

Ore chittered a hopeful question.

"Unfortunately, I'm pretty sure there's no treasure at the end of this one," Melissa answered. "But there might be some once we've done all six."

Ore considered his trainer thoughtfully, before nodding firmly at her and turning to lead the way through the Hall.

"Well, I doubt we'll get lost, at least," she said wryly to Wildfire's quiet agreement as they followed the Sableye into the maze.
