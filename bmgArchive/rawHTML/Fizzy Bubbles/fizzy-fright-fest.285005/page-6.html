Fizzytopian Shuppet and Banette

Shuppet, the Unwanted Prize Pokemon
Type: Ghost/Normal
Abilities: Super Luck/Weak Armor - HA: Oblivious
Evolves into Fizzytopia Banette via happiness

These plastic keyring toys were low tier carnival prizes almost no-one wanted. This lack of sentimentality given to them made them the perfect vessel for forgotten and forlorn spirits to inhabit. They wander places were happiness was once found and is now forgotten, striving for any sense of love they may be able to find.

Shuppet, the Patched Prize Pokemon
Type: Ghost/Normal
Abilities: Super Luck/Fluffy - HA: Cute Charm
Evolves from Fizzytopia Shuppet via happiness

While some toys are left alone and unloved, some are loved well beyond their natural lifespans. Once a high ticket prize, this frequently-patched plush became animated by a benevolent spirit. Trusting yet mischievous, these fun-loving Pokemon just want to spread their brand of impish joy to repay the love that gave them life.
