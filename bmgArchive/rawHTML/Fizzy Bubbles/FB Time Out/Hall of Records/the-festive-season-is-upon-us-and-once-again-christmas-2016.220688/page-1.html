Hear Ye, hear Ye, upon this day, the snow began to fall, unfortunately this day, the snowflakes fell upon the ground, piercing it hard and fast.

As well, a man, walked the grounds, mystical as he may be and collected these flakes into a ball.

Upon day three, this man emerged, with this
. Snow globe, containing one tiny Vanillite, a item for a young trainer to hold. Upon possession of this, it will teach one of your Pokemon a unique ability.

Frosted Flake Fall
(Ice Type)
Upon activation, the Pokemon summons a hail of razor sharp snowflakes, like its counterpart Hail, this fall of snowflakes last up to 5 turns, upon the end of each round the opponent takes damage from the flakes, a 10% more damage output than Hail due to the sharpness of the flakes.
Contest Category: Cool
Contest Effect: Makes the next two Pokémon to appeal nervous.
Appeal Points: <3 <3 <3
Jam Points: <3

I happen to know a six year old saw Santa's sleigh sparkling across the sky last night.

x2
Cranberry Juice
When your Pokémon drinks this Cranberry Juice they will learn an Egg or MT move from their natural movepool.

Super Pass will reward you with a few little perks in FB's shops on Monday.

MERRY CHRISTMAS
Snow is falling, all around me
Children playing, having fun
Its the season of love and understanding
Merry Christmas Everyone.
Today I have a selection of wonderful gifts to get you into the Christmas spirit whether you celebrate it or not. Feel free to pick up one each of the following:
1x
Christmas Pokeball (A Pokémon is released from this ball in a flurry of snowflakes). Stantler, Delibird, Snover, Deerling, or Cryogonal will receive two Beauty Points if captured with this Pokéball.)
1x
Roasted Chestnut (Raises Pokemon 3 Levels when eaten)
1x Santa Sack w/ 10,000 coins
A choice of either a
Nevermeltice OR an
Icy Rock
AND
A choice of one of these special plushies to snuggle with on a cold winter night.
Delibird Plushie ~ Gives a present which, once opened, showers your Pokémon in a mist of ice and snow, teaching them the move Blizzard. The Pokémon its used upon must be compatible with the move. Once used, it will revert into a regular plushie.
Lapras Plushie ~ When squeezed by your Pokémon it will teach them Ice Beam. The Pokémon its used upon must be compatible with the move. Once used, it will revert into a regular plushie.
Red Nosed Stantler Plushie ~ Just like Rudolf. Have a Pokemon squeeze it and it will somehow bestow that Pokemon with the knowledge to use the move Flash! The Pokémon its used upon must be compatible with the move. Once used, it will revert into a regular plushie.
I hope you all have a wonderful Christmas ^-^​
