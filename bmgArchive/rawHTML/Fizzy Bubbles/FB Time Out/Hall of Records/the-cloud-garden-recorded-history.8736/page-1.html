CLOUD GARDEN

Everybody knows that the best things for a Garden are sun and rain, so moving it closer to the sun and clouds would make a wonderful garden.
So what if we put the garden in the clouds? Imagine the beauty of this place. Actually, don't imagine, come and see for yourself, this picture of natural beauty set into the clear blues skies. Stick around and explore, who knows what surprises and adventures you will encounter.

Areas:

Stardust Fields: Updated in yellow
Ever wondered what it looks like to walk in outer space? Well chances are you'll never get a chance to do that, so why not take a walk through the Stardust Fields instead? A peaceful, uninterrupted terrain, providing habitat for all sorts of weird and wonderful pokemon, and maybe even the odd precious stone or two.

Rainbow Road: Updated in magenta
A long road that leads away from the Sky Gate which overlooks the Cloud Garden and is perfect for sight-seeing. Wild pokemon are said to appear along this path on random occasions, they seem used to people, so they won't be quick to run away when you show up. There’s a rest house somewhere along there for trainers who want to rest their tired feet and pokemon.

Cloud Forest: Updated in limegreen
A huge forest that surrounds the Rainbow Road, it's surprisingly always bright no matter what time of day it is. The forest acts as a home to most (if not all) Grass and Bug type pokemon and also several other forest dwelling types. Just try not to get lost.

Memory Lane: Updated in plum
Leading away from the Sky Gate is this seemingly endless concrete pathway. Flowers line the side of the road, laid in memory of trainers and pokemon that fought there. The saddening feel around the area attract darker, stronger pokemon.

The Fountain Garden: Updated in deepskyblue
A small garden filled with fountains, waterfalls and clear lakes. Water pokemon love this little area and thrive to the point where you can't turn around without tripping over a Magikarp. Fishing is not allowed in this area as it is considered as cruelty towards the water pokemon.

The Rock Garden: Updated in lightslategray
The main focus of this area is the amazing natural rock formations. The area is filled with holes, burrows, caves and other pokemon homes. If you have a pokemon that can Dig, there are rumours of strange and wonderful things hidden under the soil here.

Comet's Corner: Updated in red
A flat plateau that overlooks most of the Cloud Gardens. Many people say that during quiet, cloudless nights the skies are lit up in a display of shooting stars, some even say that mystical pokemon have appeared during these star storms.

Rainbow Waterfalls: Updated in aqua
A lush green area with many caves and secret hideouts. Pokemon of all kinds are drawn to the area due to its serenity and rich source of nutrition. The spray from the waterfalls create many rainbows which hang in the skies above making it a popular destination for pokemon trainers and tourists alike.

Delibird Ridge: Updated in burlywood
A nesting ground for bird pokemon. A large area of jutting rocks and crevasses where many a bird pokemon have called their home. Set into the clifftops it has become a popular spot for pokemon watchers and explorers that visit the Caverns in the mountainside.

The Oasis: Updated in darkorange
A vast, desert-like area. Nothing but sand and stone for as far as the eye can see. In the distance there seems to be a bunch of palm trees and a lake, is it real or just a mirage? Pokemon and Trainers that can brave the vicious sandstorms just might be able to find out but beware! A mysterious dense fog in said to sometimes cover this entire area and when it does all sorts of strange things begin to happen…..


So, ready to get started? The only thing left to do is choose an area to let your adventure begin. Enjoy!

A cool breeze flowed through the Cloud Forest, bringing with it the smell of blooming flowers and fresh water. Emerald light illuminated the forest, providing little shadow or darkness. The thick foliage on the canopy of the trees effectively insulated the forest, keeping the temperature of the forest at the perfect condition for the growth of the plants and Pokemon that called this region home. However, on this morning there was a being that was neither plant nor Pokemon that had penetrated the forest’s embankments.

An elderly man hobbled through the forest, leaning heavily on a gnarled stick on his left side. His clothes, a copper tunic and beige breaches, were tattered and faded and his hair a beard, both a faint silver color, were matted and greasy. Large holes were ripped in his shirt and deep wounds were clearly visible on his torso. Stumbling towards a particularly large tree, the man settles into a small seat provided the roots that lay on the green earth. Resting his chin against his chest, he closed his eyes and began to listen to the gentle sounds being produced by the forest.

The sound of wings beating quickly against the air around them alerted the man that he was not alone. Keeping his head down, he opened his eyes. From the corner of his eye he caught a glimpse of a black and yellow blur flitting between the trees.

His prey had taken the bait…

The buzz of the flapping wings grew louder and louder until the man suddenly jerked his head up from where it had rested on his chest. Mere inches from the mans face hovered an abnormally large Ninjask, frozen in mid attack with it’s sickle like forearm raised high. The man watched, amused, as all the confidence and superiority were drained from the Pokemon’s eyes and replaced by fear and uncertainty. The man’s eyes, ablaze with white light, narrowed happily upon the retreating Ninjask.

-You weren’t supposed to do that.-

The feminine voice echoed through the man’s head with a touch of disdain as a white light surrounded the man who had risen to his feet. Starting from the top of his head, the light slowly spread down his body. His body transformed under the influence of the light, his form straightening and filling out. His long hair and beard fell away, leaving a full head of blonde hair in and a blonde goatee. The wounds in his chest closed and the torn fabric mended itself as the color returned to the clothes. The years melted from his body as his body reverted to that of a nineteen year old.

The light flowed to his back and formed into a dark blue cloak with a golden clasp that lay in the center of his sternum. The clasp was solid gold and ornately carved, the images carved into the metal constantly changing showing battling creatures. The clasp hummed with energy as it absorbed the light back into it.

-You blow another chance for me to get some experience and I am going to have to beat you, Tyr.- The voice rang playfully through his head.

Looking up in time to see the Bulbasaur lower herself from the tree with her vines, Tyr smiled at her playfully.

“Ye know it is funny, Sifa”

Sifa smiled playfully at Tyr as she shook her head and gazed at him with her brown eyes.

-It was funny, the FIRST time.-

“Alright, alright. Let’s go find someone to battle then,” Tyr responded grabbing his bag from where it lay hidden behind the tree, “Besides, we need to find what we are looking for also.”

Slinging his bag over his shoulder, Tyr followed Sifa as she strolled down the path, occasionally stopping to sniff the air before continuing.
