Welcome to the Pokétreats Store! A place of mystery where berries come in and tasty, nutritious Pokémon treats go out! These treats are called Poffins (which are essentially, Pokébread), and Pokéblocks (deliciously yummy candy), which come in all kinds of colors and flavours. Treat your Pokémon to one of these delicious snacks and see what happens.

In order to make Poffins, you will need one berry to blend and then bake in our top of the range Pokéoven.

In order to make Pokéblocks, you will need at least two berries to pop into one of our wonderful Berry Blending Machines. Pop the berries in, let the machine blend them up, and wait for the completed Pokéblock to come out.

Due to lack of funds for Machine Maintenance we ask that all customers please wait three days for your Pokétreats. We will take your berry, plant it in the garden out back, wait for the plant to mature and then harvest the berries. We will use one of each berry you have handed over to us to create your Pokétreat while selling the rest to make enough money to keep our machines in excellent working order.


Rules
- You may only drop berries off on a Wednesday after which you will need to wait three days for the completed Pokétreat.
- Please ensure you post all links to all berries being used.
- If you have received a Berry via trade, I'll need the Cable Club Link which includes the link to the berry's origin so I can see where it initially came from.
- For Pokéblock creation, please clearly state which Blending Machine you wish to use and which berries you are using.
- If you want a specific Pokéblock, we've made it easier for you to see which berries make specific blocks in specific blenders. Use those as a guide, but keep in mind that if you do use random berries that are not listed in that specific category, you may not get what you want.
- Please keep the link to the Pokétreat you pick up on the following Saturday as there is a strong likelihood of many Pokétreats having varying levels for stat gains. This link will be needed when you are ready to use your Pokétreat in the PokéSpa.



Poffins​

Only the following berries may be used for creating Poffins:
Commons: Chople, Coba, Kebia, Occa, Passho, Payapa, Rindo, Shuca, Wacan & Yache Berries.
Rare: Babiri, Charti, Chilan, Colbur, Haban, Kasib & Tanga Berries.
Super Rare: Custap, Jaboca, Micle & Rowap Berries.

All Poffins give stat gains according to the rarity of the berry used.
Common Berries give a +5 stat gain, Rare Berries a +10 stat gain, and Super Rare Berries a +15 stat gain.
Depending upon the blending process, each berry can create up to two flavours of Poffin so which one comes out is entirely random.

Blueberry Poffins are Dry and increase a Pokémon's Beauty statistic.
Keep in mind that while the following berries can make a Blueberry Poffin, there is a 50% chance it will make another flavour.
Berries that can be used: Coba, Kebia, Passho, Yache, Babiri, Charti, Chilan, Kasib & Micle Berries.

Cherry Poffins are Spicy and increase a Pokémon's Cool statistic.
Keep in mind that while the following berries can make a Cherry Poffin, there is a 50% chance it will make another flavour.
Berries that can be used: Chople, Occa, Rindo, Shuca, Babiri, Charti, Tanga & Rowap Berries.

Strawberry Poffins are Sweet and increase a Pokémon's Cute statistic.
Keep in mind that while the following berries can make a Strawberry Poffin, there is a 50% chance it will make another flavour.
Berries that can be used: Occa, Payapa, Shuca, Wacan, Chilan, Haban, Kasib, Custap & Micle Berries.

Apple Poffins are Bitter and increase a Pokémon's Smart statistic.
Keep in mind that while the following berries can make an Apple Poffin, there is a 50% chance it will make another flavour.
Berries that can be used: Chople, Coba, Passho, Rindo, Colbur, Haban, Custap & Jaboca Berries.

Lemon Poffins are Sour and increase a Pokémon's Tough statistic.
Keep in mind that while the following berries can make a Lemon Poffin, there is a 50% chance it will make another flavour.
Berries that can be used: Kebia, Payapa, Wacan, Yache, Colbur, Tanga, Jaboca & Rowap Berries.



Pokéblocks​

Only the following berries may be used for creating Pokéblocks:
Common: Aspear, Bluk, Cheri, Chesto, Lum, Nanab, Oran, Pecha, Persim, Pinap, Rawst, Razz, Sitrus, & Wepear Berries.
Rare: Aguav, Cornn, Enigma, Figy, Grepa, Hondew, Iapapa, Kelpsy, Lansat, Leppa, Mago, Magost, Nomel, Pomeg, Qualot, Rabuta, Starf, Tamato & Wiki Berries.
Super Rare: Apicot, Belue, Durin, Ganlon, Pamtre, Petaya, Salac, Spelon & Watmel Berries.
Ultra Rare: Liechi Berry


Berry Blending Machines
We have three kinds of Berry Blending Machines, each one creating different kinds of Pokéblocks. Naturally, the higher the quality of the berries you use, the higher the quality of the Pokéblock you will receive. The Machines and Pokéblocks they make are listed as follows:


Berry Blender
The Berry Blender has a Base Stat gain of 10 and requires the use of two different berries in order to create the specific Pokéblocks listed below. If you use two of the same berry, you will receive a Black Pokéblock. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times. The number of points gained for stat raises are listed with each Pokéblock, but for each Rare berry used, there will be a 5 point stat bonus on the completed Pokéblock.
Common +0; Rare +5; Super Rare +10; Ultra Rare +15

Blue Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Beauty by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Chesto, Oran, Wiki, Starf
Taste: Dry

Pink Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Cuteness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Pecha, Persim, Mago
Taste: Sweet

Green Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Smartness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Rawst, Lum, Aguav
Taste: Bitter

Yellow Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Toughness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Aspear, Sitrus, Iapapa
Taste: Sour

Red Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Coolness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Cheri, Leppa, Figy, Lansat, Enigma
Taste: Spicy

Black Pokéblock
Effect: Raises one contest stat by 10
Berries Needed: Two of the same berry
Taste: Bland (No taste)


Super Blender
The Super Blender has a Base Stat gain of 20 and requires the use of three berries in order to create a Pokéblock. Specific Berries needed for making specific coloured Pokéblock are listed below. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times. The number of points gained for stat raises is listed with each Pokéblock, but for each Super Rare berry used, there will be a 10 point stat bonus on the completed Pokéblock.
Common +0; Rare +0; Super Rare +10; Ultra Rare +15

Indigo Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Beauty by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Oran, Sitrus, Bluk, Pinap, Wiki, Cornn, Starf or Pamtre
Taste: Dry

Brown Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Cuteness by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Persim, Nanab, Mago, Magost or Watmel
Taste: Sweet

LiteBlue Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Smartness by 30
Berries Needed: Aguav, Rabuta, and Durin
Taste: Bitter

Olive Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Toughness by 30
Berries Needed: Iapapa, Nomel and Belue
Taste: Sour

Purple Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Coolness by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Lum, Razz, Wepear, Leppa, Figy, Tamato, Lansat, Enigma or Spelon
Taste: Spicy


Mega Blender
The Mega Blender has a Base Stat gain of 30 and requires the use of four berries in order to create a Pokéblock. Gray, White and Gold Pokéblocks are special cases as they give multiple stat increases, so in order to make one you MUST use the berries listed below for that Specific Pokéblock. No other berry will make one. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times.
Common +0, Rare +0, Super Rare +15, Ultra Rare +20

Gray Pokéblock
Effect: Raises 3 random stats by 20 points each
Berries Needed: Ganlon, Salac, Petaya and Apicot
Taste: Three RNG rolls will determine the tastes.

White Pokéblock
Effect: Raises 4 random stats by 20 points each
Berries Needed: Spelon, Pamtre, Watmel and Durin
Taste: Four RNG rolls will determine the tastes.

Gold Pokéblock
Effect: Raises either Toughness by 70 or Cuteness & Coolness by 35 points each
Berries Needed: Two Belue and two Liechi
Taste: One RNG roll will determine the taste.


Key
Regular Font = Common Berry
Italics = Rare Berry
Bold = Super Rare Berry
Bold&Italics = Ultra Rare Berry
