Mix 'n' Match - The Pokéblock Shop

Welcome to the Pokéblock Shop! A place of mystery where berries come in and tasty, nutritious Pokémon snacks go out! These snacks are called Pokéblocks and come in all kinds of colors and flavours. Treat your Pokémon to one of these delicious snacks and see what happens.

In order to make Pokéblocks, you will need at least two berries to pop into one of our wonderful Berry Blending Machines. Pop the berries in, let the machine blend them up, and wait for the completed Pokéblock to pop out.

Due to lack of funds for Machine Maintenance we ask that all customers please wait three days for your Pokéblocks. We will take your berry, plant it in the garden out back, wait for the plant to mature and then harvest the berries. We will use one of each berry you have handed over to us to create your Pokéblock while selling the rest to make enough money to keep our machines in excellent working order.


Rules
- You may only drop berries off on a Wednesday after which you will need to wait three days for the completed Pokéblock.
- Please ensure you post all links to all berries being used.
- Please clearly state which Blending Machine you wish to use and which berries you are using.
- If you want a specific Pokéblock listed below, you will need three different types of berries listed in its category with the exception of the Gold Pokéblock which needs two of each berry as stated.
- Please keep the link to the Pokéblock you pick up on the following Saturday as there is a strong likelihood of many Pokéblocks having varying levels for stat gains. This link will be needed when you are ready to use your Pokéblock in the PokéSpa.


Berry Blending Machines
We have three kinds of Berry Blending Machines here, each one creating different kinds of Pokéblocks. Naturally, the higher the quality of the berries you use, the higher the quality of the Pokéblock you will receive. The Machines and Pokéblocks they make are listed as follows:

Berry Blender
The Berry Blender requires the use of two different berries in order to create the specific Pokéblocks listed below. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do that there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times. The number of points gained for stat raises is listed with each Pokéblock, but for each rare berry used, there will be a 5 point stat bonus on the completed Pokéblock.

Blue Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Beauty by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Chesto, Oran, Wiki, Starf
Taste: Dry

Pink Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Cuteness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Pecha, Persim, Mago
Taste: Sweet

Green Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Smartness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Rawst, Lum, Aguav
Taste: Bitter

Yellow Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Toughness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Aspear, Sitrus, Iapapa
Taste: Sour

Red Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Coolness by 10
Berries Needed: Any two of Cheri, Leppa, Figy, Lansat, Enigma
Taste: Spicy

Black Pokéblock
Effect: Raises 1 random stat by 5
Berries Needed: Two of the same berry
Taste: Bland (No taste)


Super Blender
The Super Blender requires the use of three different berries in order to create the specific Pokéblocks listed below. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do that there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times. The number of points gained for stat raises is listed with each Pokéblock, but for each Super Rare berry used, there will be a 10 point stat bonus on the completed Pokéblock.

Indigo Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Beauty by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Oran, Sitrus, Bluk, Pinap, Wiki, Cornn, Starf or Pamtre
Taste: Dry

Brown Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Cuteness by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Persim, Nanab, Mago, Magost or Watmel
Taste: Sweet

LiteBlue Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Smartness by 30
Berries Needed: Aguav, Rabuta, and Durin
Taste: Bitter

Olive Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Toughness by 30
Berries Needed: Iapapa, Nomel and Belue
Taste: Sour

Purple Pokéblock
Effect: Raises Coolness by 20
Berries Needed: Three of Lum, Razz, Wepear, Leppa, Figy, Pomeg, Tamato, Lansat, Enigma or Spelon
Taste: Spicy


Mega Blender
The Mega Blender requires the use of four berries in order to create the specific Pokéblocks listed below. Of course you can use any combination you wish, but if you do that there is no guarantee as to the kind of Pokéblock the machine will make as it tends to be quite random at times.

Gray Pokéblock
Effect: Raises 3 random stats by 20 points each
Berries Needed: Ganlon, Salac, Petaya and Apicot
Taste: Three RNG rolls will determine the tastes.

White Pokéblock
Effect: Raises 4 random stats by 20 points each
Berries Needed: Spelon, Pamtre, Watmel and Durin
Taste: Four RNG rolls will determine the tastes.

Gold Pokéblock
Effect: Raises either Toughness by 70 or Cuteness & Coolness by 35 points each
Berries Needed: Two Belue and two Liechi
Taste: One RNG roll will determine the taste.


Key
Regular Font = Common Berry
Italics = Rare Berry
Bold = Super Rare Berry
Bold&Italics = Ultra Rare Berry
