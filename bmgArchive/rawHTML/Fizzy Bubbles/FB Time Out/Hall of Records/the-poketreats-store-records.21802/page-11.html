From the other side of the Kitchen Liza could see bags of sugar, flour being flung around along with a gallon of milk, some eggs and butter and finally some berries…

What’s going on? Liza thought, wondering who exactly it was that was making her Poffins…

She no sooner had that thought when the double doors of the Kitchen swung open and a Litwick floated out, her face smothered in frosting, carrying Psychically in a tray some Poffins, Pokeblock and a nicely decorated cupcake.

“Lucy!” the trainer said astonished, “You were the one making these?”

“Lit!” she said lifting the cupcake with Psychic and handed it to her trainer to try. Liza payed close attention to detail of each and every dot of frosting along the edge, with a big swirl like center and a cherry ontop.

“These look amazing! But I didn’t know you could bake…” Liza said causing the Litwick to blush a bit.

The trainer took a great big bite and for a moment she stayed silent, Litwick floated on closer in anticipation until Liza’s eyes sparkled, “Remarkable!”

For Lucy’s first time cooking, at least in the ethereal sense, that was the best compliment she had ever gotten.

“Would you like to share the rest with your friends?”

“Lit!”

“Then let’s go!”
