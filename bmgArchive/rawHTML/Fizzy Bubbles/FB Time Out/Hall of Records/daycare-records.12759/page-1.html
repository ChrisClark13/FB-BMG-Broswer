It's tough being a Pokémon trainer. Are you busy and in need of some help? Well, we have a cure for your training blues. Every Monday you may drop off one of your Pokémon to spend a week at our luxurious facilities.

Your Pokémon will be taken care of by our expert care takers. While here Pokémon will enjoy top class food and entertainment. Give us one week and your Pokémon will look and feel stronger!

House Rules:
- You can only drop off your Pokémon on a Monday.
- You can only pick up your Pokémon on the following Monday.
- Up to 2 Pokémon per Week.
- You CANNOT have use of the dropped off Pokémon until checkout time.
- You must pickup & redeposit your Pokémon if you want it to stay another week.
- You must post your Pokémon's current level(s).
- If your Pokémon stays a full week they will grow 1 level.
- DO NOT drop off Pokémon in your PC. The pokemon must be one of your main 6 on your person.
- DO NOT post signatures.
