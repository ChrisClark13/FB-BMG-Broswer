The Shard Shop

Have a useless Shard? It can be converted into an Evolution Stone here and the rules are pretty simple.


Hand in your shard (with a link to where you received it) and you will receive a riddle. The answer is a basic Pokémon which you will be required to obtain through capture, trade or hatching (adopted Pokémon are not accepted). Return the Pokémon to me along with it's level, gender and a link to where you obtained it. You get your stone and the Pokémon goes to the Adoption Centre.

::Notes::
* Once you have handed in your Pokémon, that Pokémon no longer belongs to you so it must be removed from your active party. You do not have the right to trade it to another member after it has been handed in for the completion of your quest.
* Do not collect your stone until I have given you confirmation that the Pokémon you have handed in is the correct answer to your riddle.
* Please be sure that you definitely want to complete your quest when handing in a shard because once you've submitted it for a riddle you cannot withdraw the shard to trade it away. If you no longer wish to continue with your quest, let me know and your shard will be tossed into the Shard Shop furnace and your riddle removed from the Active Quests list.

::Shards & Stones::
Red Shard -------> Fire Stone
Blue Shard -------> Water Stone
Green Shard -----> Leaf Stone
Yellow Shard ----> Thunder Stone
Gold Shard -------> Sun Stone
Silver Shard ------> Moon Stone

Shop-of-all-Trades

Have a useless Shard, Apricorn or Shoal Item you’ve been wondering what to do with? Bring them to us and we can convert them into an Evolution Stone, Special Pokéball or Shell Bell. The rules are pretty simple.


::Shards::
Hand in your shard (with a link to where you received it) and you will receive a riddle. The answer is a basic Pokémon which you will be required to obtain through capture, trade or hatching (adopted Pokémon are not accepted). Return the Pokémon to me along with its level, gender and a link to where you obtained it. You get your stone and the Pokémon goes to the Adoption Centre.

::Notes::
* Once you have handed in your Pokémon, that Pokémon no longer belongs to you so it must be removed from your active party. You do not have the right to trade it to another member after it has been handed in for the completion of your quest.
* Do not collect your stone until I have given you confirmation that the Pokémon you have handed in is the correct answer to your riddle.
* Please be sure that you definitely want to complete your quest when handing in a shard because once you've submitted it for a riddle you cannot withdraw the shard to trade it away. If you no longer wish to continue with your quest, let me know and your shard will be tossed into the Shard Shop furnace and your riddle removed from the Active Quests list.

::Shards & Stones::
Red Shard -------> Fire Stone
Blue Shard -------> Water Stone
Green Shard -----> Leaf Stone
Yellow Shard -----> Thunder Stone
Gold Shard -------> Sun Stone
Silver Shard ------> Moon Stone


::Apricorns::
Hand in your apricorn (with a link to where you received it) and you will be given 5 random questions relating to Pokémon in general. This could be Pokémon themselves or any questions regarding the games or animé. In order to successfully convert your apricorn into one of the special balls listed below, you will need to answer all 5 questions correctly. If even one is answered incorrectly, the finished ball will be taken to the Casino where it will go up as a prize in the Daily Pick Lottery.

::Nuts & Balls::
White Apricorn ----> Fast Ball
Black Apricorn -----> Heavy Ball
Blue Apricorn ------> Lure Ball
Red Apricorn -------> Level Ball
Pink Apricorn -------> Love Ball
Green Apricorn -----> Friend Ball
Yellow Apricorn -----> Moon Ball


::Shoal Salt & Shoal Shells::
Hand in 4 Shoal Salts and 4 Shoal Shells (with links to where you have received them) and you will receive a puzzle made by me. The puzzle is pretty simple and you will have 24 hours in which to complete it. If you do not answer correctly, the completed Shell Bell will be taken to the Casino where it will go up as a prize in the Daily Pick Lottery.

::Shell Bell::
4 Shoal Salts + 4 Shoal Shells -------> Shell Bell
