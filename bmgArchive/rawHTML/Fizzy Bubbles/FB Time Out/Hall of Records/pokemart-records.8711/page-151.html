“So, it’s true...”

Standing in the middle of the Pokemart was Liza and her Pokémon Audino. After the ordeal they went through, a bond started to grow between them, her Charizard who was the shooting star that protected them from any harm was still recuperating at the Pokémon Center and wasn’t allowed to go flying long distances just yet.

Liza was planning a trip to meet up with an old acquaintance of hers, someone she had met months before.

Samantha had always had this drive to get stronger, ever since she was little. Probably even before Liza even first met Sam that fateful day many years ago...to say her Charizard and her had been through a lot together would be an understatement.

Liza had helped train Sam to be the best she could be and what the lass wanted more than anything else, was to help her partner reach heights no one had ever seen before...

That’s why she was here...

“Can I help you miss?” The clerk at the counter said as he stared blankly at the lass as she looked on mesmerized by some beautiful marble stones in a protected see through glass.

“Are those it? I mean- are these the Mega Stones everyone in town is going crazy about?” Liza asked a bit skeptical.

“Why yes, they are.” The clerk then turned towards Liza’s Pokémon companion before saying, “Are you looking to get one for your Audino?”

“Actually-” Liza turned and looked at the Pokémon next to her, “I didn’t even know Audino could Mega Evolve.” The trainer said as she looked at Audino, while her Pokémon had it’s eyes completely transfixed on a pink stone with the label Audinite written at the bottom.

“Audino?” Liza said, startling the hypnotized Normal Type, as Ashley didn’t even realize that she had been so focused on the pretty rock before her.

“What is it you want to do? What I mean is...what are you’re dreams? You’re goal, what do you life for?”

Audino thought for a moment silently, for the longest time she was just trained...not knowing what she really wanted to do herself. She felt helpless as she recalled what happened a few days before as she was first introduced to her new trainer. Watching on the side lines as Liza’s partner took on 6 Beedrills by itself and all at once...pushing itself beyond exhaustion, the fiery dragon pushed to defeat them all and with the help of the medical knowledge the Audino knew, was able to save the Charizard from further harm...

“Audino...” Ashley said, shaking her head as she didn’t know what she wanted to do or wanted to be...she just knew, she wanted to be dependable and strong, so she wouldn’t feel helpless or useless anymore.

“I see...” Liza responded as she sighed heavily. Turning toward the clerk behind the counter.

“I want to purchase a Charizardite X stone...”

The Audino looked down, saddened, turning to look at her stone one last time...

“...and an Audinite as well, please.” Liza handed over her debt card as the worker took it and charged her for the two stones she wanted to purchase.

Audino couldn’t believe it! Her stone was purchased as well.

“Whatever it is you want to be, I'll help you get there as well Audino.” Liza said as the clerk handed back the trainer’s debt card and presented the lass with a small box containing the two stones she had asked for, with Liza almost falling over as the Audino joyfully hugged her and the trainer hugged her back.

It was dark and very damp, as Ethan walked down a winding tunnel, the only thing allowing him to see was the illumination of a bright lantern as he held it up in front of him.

The sounds of digging could be heard as they echoed throughout the cave walls, as his Sableye, Adam clenched to the man's shoulder Vest for a better view and less mobility. Tearing small but sharp holes into the trainer's favorite clothes. The sound of digging getting louder and louder as they inched closer.

Eventually the duo reached a dead end as the light shone revealed the digging was coming from a very large blue dragon, the man's Garchomp, Jaws. "Anything yet buddy?" The trainer said trying not to get his hopes up as his Garchomp stopped for a second of what it was doing and shook his head in a ‘no’.

"This sucks. Are you guys sure your Mega Stones are here?" The trainer asked as his two Pokémon shook their heads in a ‘yes’ but the trainer was left a little unsure of himself, only remembering what the article he read said, ‘Pokémon that can Mega Evolve have an acute sense of where their Mega Stones are located.’ And after searching for days, the trio found a cave leading to the back of the city. Ethan figured the Stones might've been some where on the cave walls but they had been at it for almost a week and no results proved otherwise.

"I guess we should just pack it in..." The trainer no sooner said that when Jaws slammed his mighty claws into the cave's wall, finally being able to break through, with a gentle breeze of air coming from the other side!

"Did you just...I think you might've done it Jaws! Go on Adam! You help as well!" The trainer said as his Sableye jumped off of his shoulder grinning happily and he himself grabbed a shovel to the side that he had been using to help out as well, "On three! One, two...THREE!" And with three strong strikes the wall crumbled before them.

Walking through the opening, Ethan and his two Pokémon came into a dark space in the wall and right in front them, were the two Mega Stones he was looking for, as Ethan went to grab them a light above flickered on and a man to the side of the room they were standing in began yelling at them from an open door, "Freeze! And put your hands up!"

"Huh?"

"Don't you understand English? I just told you to drop the loot!"

"Wait...what?"

Inside a semi big room, with many packed boxes, some open with the logo of their local Pokémart, Ethan was being accused of breaking and entering...

"You got some guts in trying to steal from a Pokémart in broad daylight! I'll give you that! But your awfully stupid too!"

"Wait, this has to be some kind of mistake, I wasn't planning to steal...I just happened to be looking for the Mega Stones and stumbled through here..."

"If you think I'll buy tha-" the Pokémart employee said before getting cut off, "It's the truth! This is 21st Century, why would I dig a hole through a mountain just to rob a Pokémart? Surely, there are more creative ways."

"I guess that's true...but if you really aren't here to steal anything then explain to me why."

"I was looking for my Pokémon's Mega Stones, I read an article by Professor Sycamore that Pokémon capable of Mega Evolving have a way of finding their own Mega Stones, we dug for days looking for them and somehow ended up here."

"Ha! A likely story!"

"Look. If just purchasing the Mega Stones is all I have to do to get them, then I'll gladly pay for them." Ethan slowly reached for his wallet, and took out a wad of cash to the sum of 6,000 Pokedollars and handed it to the employee, who took the cash cautiously, as he slowly counted them. "It's all here alright, I guess you were telling the truth," he said, "But what do you expect me to do with this mess you made..." the employee said before looking up and finding out that the two stones, the trainer and his two Pokémon had already all fled the scene.

No, today she was here for a similar reason. She had heard through the grape vine that the Key Stone in which a trainer uses to activate Mega Evolution was being sold. With that, the final piece of the puzzle would finally come together for Samantha.

Ethan had to admit, he really didn't want to show his face again after the stunt he pulled just a couple of days ago. But if he wanted to ever get the chance to Mega Evolve his Pokémon he didn't really have much of a choice.

Standing there as he received quite an ear full from the Pokémart employee, Ethan stuck to his guns and took his punishment like a man. Cleaning and repairing the wall he broke when he was digging for Mega Stones...

"When you're all done, come to the front of the counter." The employee said as he decided to withdraw from his supervision of the young trouble maker.

Many hours later, Ethan came back to the front counter with a tired expression. It had been a long time since he had done any real hard work and he felt it in his bones!

"There you are!" The employee, Ethan later found out was the manager of the shop, shouted as he slammed his hand on the hard surface before him, "Yes sir!" Ethan jumped to attention as he replied, like a solider in the army.

"I should really never let you set foot in this store again," the man began to say before a sigh escaped his lips, "But if I'm being completely honest, you remind me of myself when I was a young rookie making my way in the world. The trouble I caused and high jinks was criminal, enough to get the attention of a few Officers every now and then..."

"Sir..." Ethan dropped his head low, he knew he was in trouble now. He was stupid in coming back, the shop keep just wanted his storage room fixed before he decided to call the cops. "I..." the trainer's words cut short before he could get the chance to apologize.

"But! I've seen it in your eyes you didn't mean any real trouble. I'm letting you off the hook, this one time." The man stuck out his hand as Ethan was slightly surprised, the trainer wiped his dirty hand on his jeans and met his own hand half way for a handshake, "Thank you! I promise not to get into any more trouble."

"I surely hope so son, I surely hope so."

As Ethan began to walk away, the manager realized he had forgotten to mention something, "Before I forget kid, the reason I asked you to come here after you were finished is..." The old man said before grabbing a package behind the counter, "If you want to Mega Evolve your Pokémon you'll need one of these bad boys," he said opening the box to reveal a box full of marbles, similar looking to the Mega Stones Ethan already had but way smaller.

"What are these pops?" Ethan felt more comfortable to say, grabbing a Key Stone to examine it.

"Key Stones- and don't call me 'Pops' I'm not that old...yet; you know those Mega Stones you purchased a few days ago, to have them work you need a Key Stone. It's a little complicated to explain but there's some research about it from an article the good Professor from Kalos explains. 'A Key Stone, a Mega Stone and a strong Bond these are the things needed to achieve Mega Evolution.'"

"Wow, so those Mega Stones are kinda useless without the Key Stone, huh?"

"Yup."

"Then I have no other choice, I'd like to purchase a Key Stone, please!" The trainer said, taking out 30 Pokedollars from his wallet to pay for it.

"I thought as much, enjoy." The employee said, taking Ethan's cash and giving him back a Key Stone in return.

With that Ethan was off. Where or how he'll be using this new found power he obtained, he wasn't too sure just yet. But life had a funny way of providing him the scene. He'd just have to wait patiently until then.
