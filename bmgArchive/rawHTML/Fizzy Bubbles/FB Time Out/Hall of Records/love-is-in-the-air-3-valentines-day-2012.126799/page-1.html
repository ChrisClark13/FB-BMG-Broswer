On this day of love and romance let your Pokemon feel special this Valentines by trying out one of our kissing booths:

Gothita Kissing Booth When your Pokemon kisses our sweet Gothita they will feel all invigorated gaining 2 levels and through the power of love will learn Psywave and Tickle. The trainer is presented with a gift basket containing 1 x Heart Scale and 1 x Gold Gummi
Purrloin Kissing Booth When your Pokemon kisses our sassy Purrloin they will feel all invigorated gaining 2 levels and through the power of love will learn Captivate and Pursuit. The trainer is presented with a gift basket containing 1 x Heart Scale and 1 x Black Gummi
Alomomola Kissing Booth When your Pokemon kisses our beautiful Alomomola they will feel all invigorated gaining 2 levels and through the power of love will learn Aqua Ring and Heal Pulse. The trainer is presented with a gift basket containing 1 x Heart Scale and 1 x Blue Gummi
Cubchoo Kissing Booth When your Pokemon kisses our cute Cubchoo they will feel invigorated gaining 2 levels and through the power of love will learn Icy Wind and Charm. The trainer is presented with a gift basket containing 1 x Heart Scale and 1 x Clear Gummi

Re: Love is In the Air <3

HAPPY VALENTINES DAY!


Why must only lovers have all the fun? Today we have a few special treats for our very special trainers and their Pokémon.​

x1 Valentine Candy ~ Gives two levels to a Pokémon with full Beauty Points from the Salon, but one level to any other Pokémon. If banked, is worth the equivalent of a regular rare candy. Full Beauty Training must be linked to when using your Valentines Candy.
x1 Love Berry Juice ~ Causes the Pokémon who drinks it to learn an Egg or MT move not yet learned. Expires 20th February 2012.
x1 Passion Pokéblock ~ +20 Beauty & Cute Stats ~ Must be nature compatible. Expires 8th April 2012.
x1 Snorunt Plushie ~ Give this cute little Snorunt plushie a big hug and it'll reward you with a fabulous 5,000 coins! Once used, it reverts into a regular plushie (Non-tradeable).

Hope all you lovebirds have a wonderful day!​
