The sight of a giant avian dragon coated by a tumultuous storm cloud was certainly a spectacle to behold, but the young trainer from Alola stood before it undaunted. He knew that this beast must be stopped alongside its treacherous master, and the Mega Altaria’s newfound Fairy typing would pose a disastrous matchup for Chryss’ Mega Sableye. Brûlée and Honey were still coming off the heels of their previous double battle, so even with their assistance, taking down the dragon might be tough. Leo called for his trusted Togetic who had been watching from the sidelines to intervene as well in hopes of delivering the finishing blow.

Chryss’ Sableye was breathing heavily from taking the brunt of many of Altaria’s attacks, and greatly appreciated as its fellow ghost type in Brûlée sprinkled some restorative Pollen Puffs on it. Meanwhile, Honey fired off a barrage of Moonblasts at the Altaria, catching its attention. The dragon screeched angrily and countered with a Moonblast of its own, a much bigger and more powerful one at that right back at the honeybee. The Altaria screeched once again and followed up with a deafening, Pixelated Hyper Voice that sent all three of Leo’s Pokemon as well as the Sableye tumbling backwards.

Brûlée quickly got back up and darted forward at the dragon, and as he approached it, the Altaria seemed to have slowed down, its movements Quashed by Chryss’ Sableye. The Mimikyu struck it with a nasty Shadow Claw, causing the Altaria to shriek angrily once again. Sensing that it was on the ropes, the bird’s shrieks turned into a chilling, yet melodic death knell, its Perish Song placing an expiration date on the battle.

There was no choice now but to end this battle as soon as possible. Leo channeled all of his willpower into his Z-Ring, causing it to radiate a pink glow. He then executed the choreography to perfection as he synced up with his partner Togetic. All of the energy generated by Leo’s Z-Ring channeled into Custard’s matching Z-Crystal, and the cherub surged with fairy type energy. Custard soared through the air towards the considerably larger opponent as he prepared to unleash a soul-crushing Twinkle Tackle. Even more energy flowed into the Togetic as he got an extra boost from Chryss’ Sableye, who cheered him on with a Helping Hand. Not to be outdone, the Altaria launched towards the oncoming Togetic with an explosion of energy building up inside of it, bracing itself for a Giga Impact of epic proportions as the normal type energy within it distorted and Pixelated. The atmosphere surrounding the battlefield exploded in lights and colors as the Togetic and Altaria rammed into each other with immense devastation of mystical fairy energy, causing everyone spectating the battle to literally see stars for a few moments.

As the dust settled, Leo rubbed his eyes to eagerly witness the result of the impact. Brûlée and Honey appeared exhausted, but were conscious as they slowly returned to Leo’s side. Chryss’ Sableye seemed to be doing fine as well, though its crystalline shield had disappeared. Leo then spotted Custard, who wasn’t moving at first, but slowly got back up to his feet and then flapped his tired wings back to his partner’s side. Lastly, the Altaria lay motionless on the ground, reverted to its natural form. The criminal leader was speechless, staring off into the distance with a piercing gaze. Resigned to his loss, he hung his head in defeat and recalled Altaria back to its Pokeball. Brûlée and Honey have each gained 1 level! Custard has gained 4 levels!

However, the leader was not yet ready to be apprehended quietly, and like his grunts he quickly held out a smoke bomb and prepared to throw it. “Not so fast!” Chryss called out, and as the bomb exploded beneath his feet, she launched a shuriken at the man. When the smoke cleared, the man was gone, but an assortment of items from his bag had been spilled onto the ground. Chryss had already returned her Sableye and was ready to run. “Thank you for all your help,” she said, turning to Leo. “Help yourself to his loot. Without your help, we might not have ever been able to track him down. And now, thanks to you stalling him, we’ve got a track on all of his and his organization’s whereabouts. I apologize for being brisk, but I must pursue him. Don’t worry if you aren’t able to catch up; he is nothing more than a cornered rat now. I will alert the scouts and finish him off. Once again, thank you.”

With that, Chryss quickly disappeared leaving only an afterimage that rapidly faded as well. Leo might have attempted to follow the fading trail as quickly as he could, but it would be hard with his exhausted Pokemon needing a moment to catch their breaths. First things first, he stepped over to the pile of items dropped from the leader’s person and picked them up. There was one Smoke Ball, as well as $500 worth of gold coins, and an assortment of ninja technique training scrolls. Upon a closer look, they seemed to be ancient predecessors to TM Thief, TM Misty Terrain, and TR Hyper Voice. As Leo pocketed the items and turned back to face his Pokemon, he was ready to make a decision about his next action. However, before any of them could move any further, the sky above them began to rumble and crack. A vortex in time opened up as the singularity began to draw to a close, and Leo knew instinctively that if they didn’t pass through it now, they might never be able to return to their own timeline.

This concludes your storyline in Dialga’s Gate. I apologize if the ending was a little rushed, but I hope you enjoyed it!
