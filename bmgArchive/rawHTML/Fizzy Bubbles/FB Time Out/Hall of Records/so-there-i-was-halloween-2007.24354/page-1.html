in the mighty jungles of Quetzlzacatenango, in search of a prized food that resides in the deepest darkest part of the jungle, all in hopes of bringing the food back to the land of Fizzby, because as you know, I am the mighty Indiana Arnie, adventurer extrodinare who once even defeated the devil himself in the mightiest of fiddle battles which has been documented throughout many history books most of which reside in the Libaries of the Twighlight Realm, you should go have a look you'll learn many new things.

But back to the adventure at hand, there I was in the might jungles, Ursaring and Nidoking attacking me from all sides, but my fellow Runpappa and Scizor defeating all that came in their path, when all of sudden we hit the deepest darkest part of the jungle, it was dark I can tell you, spookiest place i've been, a few ghosties gave me a fright when out int he distance I saw the shine of a light, getting closer and closer I saw it, and it was no light, it was the food I had longed for most, the one they call "The Merciless Pepper of Quetzlzacatenango." Just as I was about to farm them for the greater good

BANG!!!!

out came a man from the darkness, holding the pointiest and deadliest looking of spears right in front of my eyes, looknig around as best I could I tried to think of some kind of plan, asking the man why he was here he just answered that I shouldn't take what isn't mine, but you know me, I take what I want i'm Arnie everyone loves Arnie, and with that I thought why not try talk my way out of this and I just asked about him and why he lives here why he came out here and you know what he said? you knwo what he said to me?

and THAT my friends is how I managed to get the "The Merciless Pepper of Quetzlzacatenango"

and thats why, this all Hallowseve I shall be presenting to you the Hottest, the most frighteningly of peppers in the whole fizzy world, and you know what it does? Let me tell you what it does:

The Merciless Pepper of Quetzlzacatenango

Once eaten, your Pokemon will go into a full bodied hallucination where they seem to be set on fire, who knows maybe they'll be a guidance coyote or turtle i their but who knows, but once they snap out of it, Fire Shield will be their new move (can only be used on one Pokemon).

When eaten by your Pokémon The Merciless Pepper of Quetzlzacatenango will teach it the move "Fire Shield" - Fire shield creates a shield of burning fire around your Pokémon which will protect it from one elemental or physical attack. Once hit, the shield will disappear. If an opponent Pokémon makes physical contact with the Fire Shield, there is a 10% chance of it being inflicted with the condition "Burn". One use only per battle.
Category: Tough
Effect: Can avoid being startled by others.

On Halloween, there's two options a person has. To stay in the light and protect oneself. Or to join the darkness and wreak havoc! Here are two items that represent such things. Choose one and ONLY ONE. If you are seen taking both, you will get neither.

Light Barrier
Hold Item
Fling Effect: Fling becomes a Normal type defensive move with 0 power, but the user gains Reflect, Light Screen, and Safeguard affects on their side of the field. It lasts roughly twice as long as if it were normally performed.
Hold Effect: Holder cannot be critically hit.

Shadow Lance
Hold Item
Fling Effect: Fling becomes a Dark type physical attack with 75 power. The target's Reflect, Light Screen, and Safeguard are destroyed. Item returns after battle.
Hold Effect: User's attacks have +15% of critical hits.
