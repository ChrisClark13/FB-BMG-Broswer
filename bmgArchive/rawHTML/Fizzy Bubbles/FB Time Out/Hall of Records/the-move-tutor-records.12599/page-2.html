Just a reminder:

The birthday double pass can be used to get one of the following options

- 2 free egg moves

- 2 free tutor moves

- 1 free egg move and 1 free tutor move

- 1 free tutor move and 1 free egg move



If you choose you pay 1 Rare Candy while you use the pass you can obtain a 3rd Egg or Tutor move.



The one free Emerald Tutor move (and only Emerald Tutor move) does not count toward the pass or using a Candy.



It can work like this you get 1 free Emerald Tutor Move (Only for Today), you get 2 Free Moves from the Double Pass, and in addition you have the option (meaning you don't have too) of paying 1 Rare Candy for the weekly fee to obtain another move.



All rules apply.
