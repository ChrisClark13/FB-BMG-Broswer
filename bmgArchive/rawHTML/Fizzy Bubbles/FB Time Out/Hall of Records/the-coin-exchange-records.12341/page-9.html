The Coin Exchange

Welcome to the Coin Exchange! The Coin Exchange is just what the name implies - a place where you may trade in rare candies and items in exchange for coins. It’s a pretty simple process and if you know how the FB Bank works then you’ll have a rough idea how we work here, only there is one difference. Coin Exchange updates will be made regularly by either Arn or myself as soon as possible so that you may use your coins immediately. Any shops using coins will need approval before you may take the item you are wishing to purchase so to keep things as simple as possible we’ll update your balances as often as we can manage.

Rules
1) You may only exchange a maximum of 100 rare candies at a time before the exchange rate reverts to the flat rate.
2) State clearly the item/s you are wishing to exchange for coins.
3) Always await confirmation of the exchange before using your coins!
4) Rare candies may be exchanged for coins, but at this time coins my not be exchanged for candies.
5) You must post a link to where you have received any item/s you are exchanging.
6) All coins used must be reported here. When you spend them, post a link here to show where you have spent them so your balances may be updated accordingly.
7) Anyone not following instructions will have their coin balance reverted to zero, so please be careful.
8) If anyone is discovered purchasing items and not posting a link to their spending here, they will be permanently banned from using coins in FB.
9) Have fun spending!
