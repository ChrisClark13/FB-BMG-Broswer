Welcome to the Coin Exchange!​

The Coin Exchange is just what the name implies - a place where you may trade your items in exchange for coins.

Rules
1) State clearly the item/s you are wishing to exchange for coins.
2) Always await confirmation of the exchange before using your coins!
3) Rare candies and Pokédollars may be exchanged for coins.
4) Coins cannot be exchanged for candies or Pokédollars.
5) You must post a link to where you have received any item/s you are exchanging.
6) All coins used must be reported here. When you spend them, post a link here to show where you have spent them so your balances may be updated accordingly.
7) You should have only ONE post in between updates. Altering a post prior to an update is not allowed, but if you have multiple transactions between one update and the next, then you should edit the one post with all transactions. Not doing so will ensure all deposits are ignored, and a possible ban placed upon you in all shops where coins are used.
8) Anyone not following instructions will have their coin balance reverted to zero, so please be careful.
9) If anyone is discovered purchasing items and not posting a link to their spending here, they will receive a temporary Game Corner ban.
