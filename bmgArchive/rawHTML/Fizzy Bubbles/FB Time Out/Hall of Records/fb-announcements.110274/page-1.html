It turns out an Admin was deleting old, unused, usergroups and didn't realise the FB Usergroup was current.

Unfortunately it couldn't been restored, but it has been recreated, so to rejoin all you need to do is go to your Settings > Permission Groups > Fizzy Bubbles Member, then follow the prompts. Arn, Arc, or I will then come along and add you. If you're not a Bulbagarden FB Home Boarder please ensure your account name post is up-to-date on your appropriate home board (UPN, Serebii) or you won't be added. Anyone can sign up with a name you use on another forum claiming to be you, so unless you post under your own username on your own home board stating what your username is here, we can't be assured it is actually you applying to the usergroup.

Due to an unfortunate and unforseen consequence of Bulbagarden's change from vBulletin to XenForo, a permissions glitch affecting our usergoup means we are unable to add new people. It seems that anybody not in the usergroup at the time of the software change is locked out and is unable to post. This, combined with a couple of other issues that will no doubt be fixed in due course leaves us with little choice but to temporarily shift the Bulbagarden focus to UPN and Serebii.

For now the Casino and TCG Corner will return to UPN, and any returning Bulbagarden members currently unable to post in FB will be shifted to either UPN or Serebii until the permission issues are resolved. This of course means that FB's BMG Registration thread will also need to close for the time being.

Hopefully it will be sorted out soon, and all will return to normal.

Posting now to advise that the permissions issue has been fixed with a new FB usergroup set up today.

To apply simply mouse over your name at the top right of the page (next to Inbox and Alerts), and select "Join User Groups" from the menu then apply to the "Fizzy Bubbles Member" group. An FB Mod will then approve your application so make sure we know who you are if you haven't already posted in the UPN or Serebii Account Names threads.

For BMG Home Boarders who are temporarily using another board you can now resume posting here. Any transactions you have in the Candy Store, Pokébank, and Coin Exchange since it was last updated can be moved to their corresponding shops here, while Pokémon in shops can be picked up tomorrow (Monday) from the shop they are currently in, and dropped off in the BMG FB shops.

If you have any questions please feel free to PM me.
