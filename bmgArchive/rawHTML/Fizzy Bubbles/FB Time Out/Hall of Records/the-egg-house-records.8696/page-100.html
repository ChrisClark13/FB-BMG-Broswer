Roughly…about a week ago…

December 24th, 2014.

It was a day before Christmas and all through the base not a Pokémon was string, well one actually, a cute little Budew!!

Everything around the base had been decorated with loving care. Stockings were hanging over the fire place, as the tree sat near the corner with hundreds of twinkling lights; while a young man, about age 22, was seating on his couch wrapping some last minute presents with his Budew.

"How are you enjoying the festivities?" the boy asked as he put another gift underneath the tree with the rest of the pile.

"Budew!" the Rose Bud chimed, happily sporting a warm Red Scarf.

"I never thought I’d have so many Pokémon when I was a child," the boy commented as he looked at the pile of presents for his team as he grabbed a cup of hot chocolate from the coffee table, sipping it slowly as he looked out the window.

Snow had been falling for about a 2 weeks now, but it wasn't like there was any where he would rather be then his own home with his Pokémon.

"Budew?"

Just then, a cold gust of air past through the structure all of the sudden, the flames in the fire place went out.

"Budew!" the grass type began to panic, "Don’t worry Rosa, it’s probably some pile of snow that fell, I’ll get it working again," the trainer said as he realized why his Pokémon was panicking, it might have been dark but he could still make out the shadow darting this way and that with the illumination of the tree's lights.

"HEY YOU!! WHAT DO YOU THINK YOUR DOING IN OUR HOME!!" Ethan yelled out as he tried to get the jump on the big fat shadow, "Ooof!" Ethan winced in pain as he hit the wood flooring. And just like it had gone out, the flames on the fireplace seemed to have suddenly ignited once more like magic.

With everything bright once again, Ethan found what he had grabbed was nothing more than a pillow from his couch.

"Was I imagining things?" he asked his Budew who was jumping up and down, with one of her petals pointing toward the window and when Ethan turned around to look no body would believe; a large red sleigh being pulled by 7 flying Stantlers, a big fat guy in red suit at the driver seat and a gigantic bag riding next to him full of packages.

Instantly thinking the worst, Ethan scrambled and ran over to the presents he had wrapped, counting them to make sure none of them had been taken...instead he found one more present had been added to the bunch. An oval shaped one wrapped nicely in a red ribbon addressed to him, with a card that read:

DO NOT OPEN TILL THE 31st OF DECEMBER,

Sincerely, S.C.
--

A week had past and Ethan returned to the Egg House with the Egg he was left with on Christmas Eve. He waited around for an attending to help him, before placing the Fire Core Egg on the counter…it took about two hours more than usual but it was finally time for the egg to hatch! He had half expected Santa to give him some coal but instead…when the egg hatched the boy just couldn't believe his eyes, the last Pokémon egg of the year had been the Pokémon he had spent all this time looking for!!

"Ruff, ruff, ruff!" the dog Pokémon, Growlithe yipped happily wagging her tail at her trainers feet.

With a tear streaming down the trainer’s face, Ethan picked up his new born Fire Type Pokémon. Hugging her in a warm and cuddly embrace, her fur felt like a cloud directly brought from the heavens.

"Thanks to you I can finally stop hatching Fire Eggs!" the trainer chuckled, "NO MORE: Numels, Torkoals or Slugmas-"

"Hey Ethan, weren't you supposed to hatch Fire Eggs until you got a certain Pokémon, for you know who?" a Gastly said appearing out of nowhere.

"Well, actually I’ll still fulfill my promise," Ethan said looking at his new Pokémon, "but in a different way."

"Growl?" the Growlithe tilted its head to the side with an inquiring expression on her face.

"Well, in time at least. Now then, how about you and I go play catch at the park! Haven’t done that in a long time, but we do need to give you a name…how about…Belleme?"

"Growlithe!!"

"I’ll take that as a yes," the boy chuckled as his dog Pokémon began to lick his face affectionately.

Walking outside the door, the boy, his dog and ghost friend decided walking home would be best for a bright sunny day as today…

OoC: For such a special belated Christmas miracle, I’ll be putting my Female Growlithe in this Christmas Ball, thanks Stark!

As per usual, Ethan entered the egg house with an egg in his arms, and high hopes in his gut that another Pokémon worthy to call a team member was going to be born today. Carefully he placed the egg on the counter before waiting for an assistant to come around and well, assist with hatching the egg if there were any complications.

The egg shook violently before spinning rapidly in place as two scythes ripped through the shell causing it to fall apart and an emerald green bug Pokémon emerged from within. The insect picked up its head after showing its display of power, content with its work he looked up before meeting its eyes with its new trainer. For a moment the Scyther stayed motionless looking into the trainer’s eyes so intently it was as if it was looking into his very soul, before bowing respectfully and presenting its right scythe to his chest.

"Scyther, Scy, Scyther."

"James be a pal, and translate for me?" the trainer asked his Gastly.

"He says that you have been through quite a lot in your life for someone so young, he senses great things from you and will happily do whatever you ask of him, all he wants in return is to help him grow."

Ethan nodded before walking to his bowing subject placing both of his hands on the Scyther’s shoulders before helping him get up, "Rise, I don’t need a servant, what I need is a partner and a friend. You will be both! I see great things in you as well, and as a show of our bond, I will name you Razor." The boy finally said retrieving a Pokeball 7/10 from his belt and pressing it against the Scyther’s head before placing him inside.

"Hey Ethan, don’t you already have a Scyther?"

"No point in having two…although…NAH one is enough."

"Well which one will you keep?"

"I think I’ll give Razor a chance, actually, he isn’t as show offish as the other one."

And with that Ethan and James left to go on a little training trip, with their new Swordsmon.
