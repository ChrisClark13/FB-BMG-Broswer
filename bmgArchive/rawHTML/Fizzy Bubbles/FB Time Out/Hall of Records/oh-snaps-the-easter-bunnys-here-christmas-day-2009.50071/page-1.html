Merry Christmas!

MERRY CHRISTMAS!​

Now that we're approaching the end of a decade let's see what we cand find *rummages around in her santa sack*

This sweet and succulent Cranberry will raise your Pokémon's level by 2.
This Cranberry Memory Berry will allow the Pokémon who eats it to pluck information from its own DNA to help it relearn four moves it has forgotten through evolution, or to learn four random egg moves it wasn't born with. In some instances, your Pokémon may even learn a combination of Relearned and Egg Moves.
This Super Pass will allow you a few extra little perks on Monday, and finally...
This Trading Card Palace Pass will allow you to choose any cards you like to the value of 75,000 coins from any of the currently available Booster Packs and Theme Decks on offer.

Hope you enjoy, and have a wonderful day!

MYSTIC MISTLETOE

~Both Pokemon gain 2 Levels each
~Both receive an increase in Beauty Levels of 2
~The Beauty Contest Stat of each Pokemon raises by 20
~The Male Pokemon will suddenly learn the move Lick and the Female Pokemon the move Sweet Kiss.

Also in the bag is:

1 x Heart Scale
1 x Christmass Coin Case w/1000 coins
