Quickly recognising the light now surrounding your Makuhita, you shout encouragment along with commands to help fuel the process further. Even as the words echo through the chamber Suméo begins to undergo rapid changes; his normally pudgy body quickly gaining even more mass and bulk than you'd anticipated, seeming to push his prostrate position into a hefty hulk. The knot atop his head appears to vanish, replaced by a swirling formation almost similar to a cloak extending from the back of his noggin. Where his spherical hands extended from his short arms now lie threateningly wide palms the size of your head, and by the time the light fades gone is the childlike wrestler you used to know; pushed by the battle a Hariyama now stands in Suméo's place, flexing his hands that ripple with new muscle.

"Foolsssss! Do you think a paltry evolution will be sssssufficient?!" hisses Arbok. "Now die!!"

"Chroma, Double Team post-haste!" orders Yuanli, and by the severity of both master's and enemy's tones the Sableye once again springs into action. You spy Chroma scuttle along the rocky floor, soon followed by two -- then three, then four. Spreading out as Houndour comes bounding at them, the group of Chroma leave the charging canine with its fangs bared headed for the sage, only for the old man to promptly swing the tip of his staff forward. With a dull thud the wooden point meets the Houndour by the skull on its forehead, stunning it enough to stop it in its tracks much to your amused observation.

"Will, now! Use your focus; we can't keep this gambit up forever!" calls the veteran, reaching into his pouch for a few red berries. "Ha! An Occa Berry for you, young one!" he cries, flinging the fruit into Houndour's Fire Fang; the resulting contact releasing a small explosion of spice enough to make your eyes water at the edges as the Fire-resistant berry weakens the attack. "Chroma, Fury Swipes to Detect! Exercise utmost caution!"

"SABL SABL SABL!" Without your attention on the gremlins, it surprises you as Team Chroma unlatches themselves from the roof of the chamber, dropping onto Arbok who reacts with an enraged roar, tearing at it with repeated notches all over its skin. A glob of Sludge Bomb flies over you, followed by another that nearly grazes Medéo by the side, but you know you have no time to waste on seeing if Chroma's evasive methods are effective. Cornered like a rat, Sneasel glares at the two fully evolved Fighting Pokémon before charging ahead; its hand glinting with a harsh Ice Punch that Suméo takes -- with even more weight and fat to take the physical blow, Suméo barely even seems to register the technique before he smacks his Belly like a Drum, forcing his very essence into physical strength...

"Sneaaaaasel!" hollers the ice feline, an evil glint in its eye that both you and Suméo catch, and before you realise it Arbok and Houndour have relinquished their quarries as they advance over to Suméo. A strike, joined by another, then combined with a series of smacks as you recognise the Beat Up strategy in horror; having taken the most damage, would Suméo make it?

"Medicham!" A valiant cry comes from Medéo as he finishes his Psych Up, all of Suméo's battle power now his as well, breaking into a quick jog just as the snake and the dog disperse. While Suméo staggers in recovery Medéo wastes little time in a direct Force Palm to Sneasel's chest; the undiluted power of the Fighting strength unlocked by the Medicham sending the Sneasel into the dirt as a screaming projectile. Barely before it has a chance to recover Suméo has already forced himself upon Sneasel, swinging both his hands in a spectacular slam upon it, barely letting a squeal escape as both his magnificent palms cover the Ice-type in the show of Close Combat. Lifting his limbs in a scattering of dust Suméo reveals Arbok's underling, in no shape at all to continue fighting.

"Will. Now that Suméo's just evolved, my guess is that he's tired himself out," Yuanli points to you tersely; indeed the Hariyama appears to support himself on one huge hand. "I think you know as well that Belly Drum and Close Combat are skills that drain the user considerably as well, in exchange for unimaginable power... it might be suitable to pull Suméo out to a safe zone temporarily. Arbok and Houndour, noting this, will likely target him first."

What will you do?

Inside Jirachi’s Casino a boy dressed in a black jacket and black trousers sat in front of a slot machine, hammering away, while a small Meditite merrily collected all the coins its trainer had won in a little bucket. The manager of the place, a young woman with shiny, waist-length black hair and emerald green eyes cheered for her customers via a microphone, exclaiming that the more Pikachus one got in a row, the more coins one earned. A bell clanged loudly nearby, signaling that one lucky patron had just won the jackpot, and the dazzling room was suddenly flooded in a wave of celebratory roars and screams.

Feeling good about their current win streak, the boy and the mystic left their slot machine, strode over to a Roulette table, and cheerfully dumped out their pail of coins in front of an astonished casino dealer. The two friends shared an eager smile and excitedly shook hands before the boy announced his bet. “Everything on Red Togepi!” he confidently exclaimed, as the Meditite grinned and nodded its onion-shaped head in agreement. With that, the other two gamblers at the table, a lad wearing a bright yellow Souwester hat and tattered overcoat, and a Collector wearing a Runpapa-esque sombrero, gaped their mouths and sweatdropped at the duo’s enthusiasm.

Moments later however, the boy and his Meditite companion sat in glum silence on the frontsteps of Jirachi’s Casino. The brown-haired youth was as still as a Silcoon awaiting evolution. Had it not been for the rise and fall of his shoulders accompanying an occasional sigh, passersby would have certainly mistaken him for a statue. At his feet lay an empty coin case. Overhead, a massive animatronic model of the Wish-granting Pokemon waved a hand hypnotically, beckoning gamers to enter and test their luck. Outlined in bright multicolored bulbs, the club’s mascot bathed the terrace and the forlorn pair in iridescent neon light. Chin in hands, elbows on knees, grey-green eyes staring at nothing in particular, the boy sat lost in thought, wondering if "fortune would ever guide his hand."

* * *​

In the Catacombs deep beneath the surface of Mireno, the trainer Will Hale grinned broadly at the thought that his gamble had paid off. For the first time in his life, Lady Luck had smiled on him. The Belly Drum-Close Combat combo and teamwork strategy he ordered had worked, and with only Houndour and Arbok left, they now had numbers on their side.

Thinking hard to come up with a plan, Will massaged his forehead and turned to his partners. Glancing at Sumeo, the boy marveled that it had evolved into the mighty Hariyama during its first ever battle and reasoned that being united in determination towards the same goal as Medeo and Chroma had caused it to reach its final stage so soon. Sumeo grinned happily at Will and took another bite of its Leftovers, trying to replenish its spent strength. Returning the smile, Will noticed that despite its cheerful exterior, Sumeo labored to get a full breath. Will realized that its evolution, combined with the two energy-sapping attacks, must have drained it considerably.

“Fall back, mate!” Will directed, motioning for the hulking sumo wrestler to retreat and take a break. “Medeo, cover him with Reflect, then follow Chroma’s lead with Double Team!” The Medicham nodded and elegantly hopped from foot-to-foot readying itself for battle. Recalling Yuanli’s admonition to stay focused, Will suddenly had an idea and instantly called out further orders. “While our enemies are distracted by the clones, look for an opening then hit Houndour with a Focus Punch!”
