This rewards thread exists to thank those of you who put in the time and effort to work FB. Without you we can't have zones, and without zones there is no FB. Since you take time out of your personal lives to provide our members with adventures, it's only fair you should be rewarded in some way.

Rewards are offered in the form of points which can then be used to convert into another form of currency, or to purchase specific prizes. One point is the equivalent of one dollar.

Each update of 300+ words will allow you to claim 50 points per person updated. It does not matter if the one update consists of 300 words or 3,000 words, the reward is still the same. Updates only need to be long enough to advance the story, there is no need for epic adventures all the time. Updates of less than 300 words are rewarded on a sliding scale.

If all updaters write 300+ word updates, and they have a group of five members which is what the norm seems to be these days, then you're looking at an updater receiving 250 points ($250 if converted into cash) per set of updates which is the equivalent of one zone reply.

Please note that REPOSTS DO NOT COUNT as an update. In fact, we advise against reposting updates at all. A better idea may be to link the member to their last update instead. Please also note that we'd rather not see endings such as, "What will you do?" included in the update's word count.

Trainer Battle Referees will now be rewarded using the same word count system as a zone updater and will be able to claim their rewards here on a monthly basis rather than in the Trainer Battle Results thread at the conclusion of the battle.

TCG Refs can claim 50 points per reffing. While their posts may differ from a long and wordy trainer battle reffing, they do have other behind-the-scenes factors to take into account, such as keeping track of both player's decks and ensuring the game plays out properly.

Contests will be returning in the not-too-distant future which will mean the return of the co-ordinator. Coordinators will most likely also claim based on word count, we can't say yet for sure and will revise the situation when the time comes.

Updaters from UPN, Serebii and Bulbagarden will all be posting here at Bulbagarden to receive their points, and after everyone has posted a Mod will confirm your rewards so which are then recorded in the Point Reserve.
