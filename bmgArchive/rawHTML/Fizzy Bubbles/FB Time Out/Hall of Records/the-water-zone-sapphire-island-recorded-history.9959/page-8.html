Emerald Jungle: Please reply in LimeGreen

Joshua: As Hayley blasts a Flamethrower into the sky, Bellossom watches in awe and turns to you, a glimmer of hope shining in her eyes. “Very well, I shall come with you!” She exclaims excitedly. She turns to another Bellossom and nods to it, the other Bellossom nods in return and takes the rest of the pokemon away to a safe place. Oddish wriggles about in your arms as Bellossom motions you to follow her. You do so and after about 5 minutes of walking, your surroundings drastically change. The sun no longer shines through the trees and you realise instantly that you must be within the Vileplume’s territory. Looking up to the tress, a thick layer of Stun Spore floats above you and you cover your mouth and nose to protect yourself from breathing the lethal powder in. Bellossom begins to cough, not used to this harsh environment. Oddish, who is bundled up in your arms looks up to you and says, “Oddd..ish?” It seems that she doesn’t like being here. All is quiet until you hear a loud voice, you all run to a bush and peer through it and you are greeted by the sight of a lot of Vileplume, Oddish’s with purple leaves and some Gloom with a man standing on a platform in front of them. He is adorned with a huge cloak covered in the lush jewels that are found in this part of Sapphire Island. You begin to eavesdrop and you hear the words, “And now, we’ll destroy those pathetic pokemon of the Bellossom clan and take over the Emerald Forest! It has been our dream since I first arrived here!” All of the pokemon cry out enthusiastically as you feel an anger take over you. Bellossom gasps quietly and begins to cry, fearing for her group. What will you do?

Dark Scizor: Deciding that Neptune may not be able to muster the Pig Monkey’s next attacks, you summon Scizor to finish off the battle. In a flash of red, he stands tall, the sun which shines through the trees reflecting off his majestic red metal body. Speaking your orders, he keeps a tight focus on Mankey and nods solemnly. His wings begin to flutter as he takes to the air, with Mankey staring him down determinedly. As Mankey leaps up to attack Scizor with a Rage attack, Scizor’s wings glow a bright white as he slams in to Mankey and deals heavy damage. Scizor then powers up his right claw, it glows silver as he smashes Mankey who crashes to the ground, KO’ed (Mankey is capturable). Scizor then lands on the ground and eyes the small Pig Monkey, who doesn’t make a move. All is silent in the forest until you hear a loud screech. Both you and Scizor look up into the sky trying to find the cause of the noise. Until you hear a grunt and a thump.. And a Primeape stands on a tree stump in front of you. Unusual for a Primeape, he is stood quite still, but you see his eye twitching lightly. He pounds his fists together as he stares at you directly, you feeling unsure of how to react. Scizor raises his claws, taking a defensive stance as Primeape points at you, not even murmuring a sound. Suddenly a gust of wind flies through the forest and you could swear that you could hear the word “Enma…” in its howl. You find yourself unable to take your eyes away from Primeape’s and he stares at you directly, and you feel a shiver fall down your spine and land in your toes. Gulping, you wonder what he heck is going on. How will you react?

P-Arts: Nidoqueen raises her arms in triumph as Jiroubi is returned to the safety of the Pokeball. Letting out a roar, she glares directly at you as you release Necross into the Emerald Forest. The 30foot Steelix filling the area quite easily, he releases a tremendous roar which causes the forest to shake. Shouting out your commands, Necross nods and his eyes flash a dark crimson red as his usual calm manner transforms into that of a blinded rage. He roars once more as he swings his huge tail and slams it into Nidoqueen who is sent hurling backwards. She slams into a tree and knocks it down, holding her head she stands up and an orange aura surrounds her as she uses her SuperPower, approaching Necross and slamming into him, knocking him back. However Nidoqueen’s counter attack enrages Necross further and a red aura engulfs his huge body as his attack power is heightened. Letting out an intense roar yet again, he makes his way across the ground, his immense weight causing the ground to shake, Nidoqueen looks up helplessly as he slams his midriff into her torso, winding her and sending the Poison behemoth hurling backwards landing on her back, but she doesn’t get up. This sight causes Necross’ rage to diminish and he takes a deep breath, his mindset returning to normal.

Nidoqueen struggles to get up and then flexes her arms, cracking the joints back into place. Necross then curls into a tight ball as best he can and begins to roll around for a few moments to build up momentum, he then approaches Nidoqueen, his Rollout attack causing all obstacles in his way to be hurled to the side as if they weighed as much as a feather. Nidoqueen braces herself and places her arms in front of her, and waits for Necross to impact. She glows a bright white as she summons as much energy as she can for her Strength attack. Necross hits her full on and she roars out loud as she slams her arms into his body, holding the huge beast back with an impressive display of Strength. She then realises that her arms can’t hold out and is crushed as Necross’ weight falls over her, whipping up a cloud of dust. As the dust clears, Necross has managed to stop himself and Nidoqueen is laid on the ground, panting heavily, she slowly gets up and raises her arms, roaring. Turning his head, Necross nods to you as he waits for his next orders. What will you do?
