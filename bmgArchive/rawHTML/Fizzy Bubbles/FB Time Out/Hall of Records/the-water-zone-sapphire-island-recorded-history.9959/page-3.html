Lapis Lake:

Joe: Plusle, pretty pissed off, tries to paralyze Lotad with a Thunder Wave, but fails as she is knocked out of the lake by an Astonish attack. She lands on her back as Lotad stays in the water and begins to summon a huge Surf wave! Plusle manages to get up and uses Quick Attack, taking her out of the Surf wave’s path and she smashes into Lotad, knocking the lily pad pokemon back further in the water. Lotad, then begins to growl at Plusle, which in turn will prevent her attacks from causing too much damage. Lotad then summons another Surf as Plusle stands back, looking at you, waiting for your commands. What will you do?

SkepticReptile: After deciding to withdraw Swablu to have a little calm down, you send Treecko to battle Corphish. Corphish, angry after his small bicker, now is even angrier that you returned Swablu and he then begins to start shouting obscenities at Treecko! This Corphish sure has a temper and he then stares right back at Treecko as he is Leered. Corphish is then caught off guard as Treecko begins to pound the crab with his tail! Corphish then uses Harden and his entire body hardens, raising his defense. Treecko, realising that his efforts are futile, then stops and jumps back as Corphish fires a barrage at Bubbles at Treecko, hitting him for average damage and knocking him back slightly. Corphish then uses Harden once again, to reinforce his defences. What will you do?

Ocean & Reefs:

ZoraJolteon: Slowqueen begins to send her psychic message towards Wooper. As you hold your head in pain, Wooper only receives a faint message but gets the idea; attack Pelipper. Wooper’s small body begins to glow with Rock energy as she delivers a hard blow on Pelipper’s huge beak. Pelipper cries out in pain as it drops Wooper towards the sea! You and Slowqueen move towards where Wooper will land.. But 2 Wingull divebomb down and catch Wooper in their claws.. They then lift Wooper back up to Pelipper who looks down at you and frowns.. It then opens it’s beak and an orb of water forms, it then fires a Hydro Pump down towards you and Slowqueen at a fast rate. What will you do?

Thibideau: Holding onto Pandora, a huge bubble surrounds you both as you submerge into the ocean, and head down towards the Reefs. You admire the immense beauty of the reefs, the sun shining through the water and emphasising the colours of the reef. Pandora pulls you deeper into the depths so you can get a closer look at the reefs. Whilst exploring in amongst the reefs, you see a stream of bubbles floating upwards.. As you swim towards them, you are shocked to find that it is a boy, unconscious with a KO’ed Shellder floating next to me. In panic, you look about and spot a school of Lanturn and Chinchou, it seems that they attacked the boy. How will you react?

Forever Flygon: Releasing Nookie to keep an eye on you, you additionally release Mikozou to support you whilst you swim. Nookie swoops above you as you and Mikozou begin to swim out into the Ocean. You look below you and see schools of Remoraid, Magikarp and Chinchou swimming in the depths. After about 30 minutes of swimming with Mikozou, he seems to think that you are above the reefs. Mikozou then begins to dive, a huge bubble surrounds you both as Nookie stays put above the water. You slowly submerge and look about, you pass through a school of Magikarp, they don’t even flinch as the two of you pass through them. You eventually reach the reefs and you admire their beauty. The different colours are emphasised by the sun shining through the water. You both swim inbetween them until you come across a cave.. And a line of Corsola swimming into it. Will you go there, or will you continue exploring the reefs?

Turquoise River:

Torkoal Stu: After looking up Fearow’s statistics, you decide to use Blackrose to teach that huge bird a lesson. Releasing her into the River, her huge form fills most of the river as she looks down at you, happy to be in her first ever battle. You smile back at her, completely oblivious to her quite intimidating form.. After issuing your attacks, Blackrose then looks up at Fearow and then fires a white burst of energy which strikes Fearow’s wing. You see that the wing has blackened, signalling that Fearow has been burned. Due to this Fearow cries out in pain as it begins to lower slightly, it’s wing causing it considerable pain. Goldeen then panics even more as Fearow then uses Façade and dives towards Blackrose, striking her and knocking her back slightly. Blackrose roars in pain as Fearow swoops round and filled with rage, then pecks at Blackrose, who is unable to move due to being pecked at repeatedly; Fury Attack! Fearow still has a tight grip on Goldeen. What will you do?

Ythrelancus: Along with Axtan, you decide to head to the Turquoise River. After a small while of walking, you arrive at river, which feeds the nearby Lapis Lake. You admire the beauty.. Today the sun is shining brightly and the river is lit up with schools of Magikarp swimming along the bottom of the river. You then take a seat on the bank and shield your eyes from the bright sun. Axtan sits by your side and seemingly stares into nothingness. The area is so peaceful, and you slip off to sleep. A few minutes later, you are rudely awoken by a Water Gun to the face! You sit up in a shock and look towards the River. Looking back at you is a Remoraid, who then blasts Axtan with a Water Gun. Looks like it wants a battle.. What will you do?

Emerald Jungle:

P-Arts: After Jiroubi’s little hissy fit, and your casual beating. You decide to watch out for whatever the creature was that attacked Jiroubi. Both of you feel a sense of paranoia.. That someone is watching. You hear a rustling in the trees, and Jiroubi turns and stares at the leaves, trying to Leer whatever it is out. Suddenly, a flash of blue appears next to Jiroubi and smacks him with a flurry of kicks and then disappears again. Jiroubi, even more annoyed by this, uses Rock Tomb and throws rocks all over the area. A yelp is heard as a very hostile looking Nidoran comes out from within the bushes.. She looks at you with an angry twinkle in her eye, her teeth bared and a hiss emitting from her tiny mouth. Jiroubi then charges his fist with fire energy and launches himself towards Nidoran, who merely take the attack, but then counters Jiroubi’s Fury Swipes by firing Poison Stings at his paws, each one piercing his skin. Jiroubi then staggers back, the Poison slowly taking effect. Nidoran growls at you fiercely.. What will you do?

Joshua: Michael runs forwards and stops just short of the Stun Spore and then begins to communicate with the Oddish. Oddish then looks at you and tilts it’s small head and replies “Oddis..h..odd ..odd ish!” Michael then looks at you and motions you to come closer.. You do so and Michael rubs his head on your leg, to show Oddish that you are friends with him, and not controlling him. Oddish then smiles gently and shakes it’s leaves. A single emerald falls on the ground and Michael runs towards it and picks it up and places it on his fur, it shines beautifully. Oddish then runs off down the path and Michael then nods at you and runs off. You chase after him and Oddish and soon find yourself standing in front of hordes of Oddish and Gloom. They look at your wearily and one of Gloom steps forwards and points at you, “Gl-oommmm..” It then looks at the Oddish you just encountered and frowns. It seems that Gloom isn’t happy that you came to their Emerald Jungle sanctuary, and wants you to prove yourself.. By a 1-on-1 battle with this Gloom.. What will you do?

Jade Swamp:

Sasuke-san: With Ino perched on your shoulder, she adamantly decides on your destination to the Jade Swamp. After a short walk, you stand still and take a look around. The ground you stand on is soft and is covered in sinkholes all around the Jade Swamp. The smell is bearable, yet you still feel the urge to cover your nose. It’s quiet, apart from the bubbling of the Swamp. Today, the Swamp’s water levels are slightly elevated, so the swamp water spills over the bank. Trying to think of something to do in this putrid environment, a noise can be heard. You look towards the center of the Swamp, where bubbles are forming.. Slowly a form rises from the murky, thick waters.. A turquoise body.. Covered in swamp water, with huge jaws and teeth..On it’s head is a crown made from rocks and vines.. It then roars it’s most ferocious roar and says “Toto-d-ile, toto?” and looks at you. Seems this Totodile sees herself as “Queen of the Swamp“. After checking you out for some time, you begin to approach her slowly, taking care not to fall into the sinkholes that surround the swamp. Totodile then looks at Ino, and begins to shout at her. Swiftly, Totodile then jumps out of the Swamp and fires a Water Gun at Ino and knocks her off your shoulder. She lands with a splat on the ground as Totodile laughs and jumps into one of the sinkholes. What will you do?

Suicide: As you issue your attacks to Ledian, Whiscash listens intently, whilst keeping an absolute dopey look on his face. Ledian then releases an ear-drum bursting Screech, Whiscash at first, begins to flinch but then lets his body sink into the swamp, significantly lowering the effectiveness of the Screech. Whiscash then re-emerges and fires a Water gun at Ledian who counters with Psybeam, which cancels out Water Gun and hits Whiscash. As Ledian approaches Whiscash to use Mega Punch.. Whiscash extends his whiskers and begin to Tickle Ledian. Ledian bursts out laughing as Whiscash then fires a Water Gun which smacks Ledian in the torso and sends her flying backwards. What will you do?

Manji: Not very impressed by Poliwag’s sneak tactics, you send Bandit after him. Bandit, reluctant at first, gives in and then dives into the ground next to where Poliwag disappeared down the sinkhole.. Bandit tries his best to sense where Poliwag is, but fails as Poliwag emerges in front of you and giggles. Then dives into the hole that Bandit just dug. You hear a lot of pokemon-language obscenities coming from Bandit, and a lot of giggles from Poliwag. Bandit then bursts from the hole, Poliwag ontop of his fist. Poliwag then lands and frowns as Bandit laughs back at Poliwag, who then shoots a barrage of Bubbles at Bandit, knocking him back. Poliwag’s stomach then begins to glow, preparing to put Bandit to sleep with Hypnosis. What will you do?

Latias10: Ledy begins to talk with Surskit, who stops just in front of Ledy. Ledy smiles as Surskit nods and smiles back. Ledy turns to you and gives a Ledian-style thumbs up. Surskit then looks at your bound feet and looks puzzled, then tells Ledy something. Ledy then grins and approaches you, and points at your feet and says, “Le-le-di-an-le-dian” Ledy then grabs your shoulders and tries to pull you out. You try to aid Ledy by tugging your leg.. After a minute of pulling, pop! One of your legs is freed. You ensure that your free foot doesn’t sink as you manage to free the other. Ledy then leads you over to a safe spot of ground for you to stand on. Surskit then comes up to you and smiles, and begins to skate once again.. After a few minutes, the ground begins to rumble. You steady yourself, but Surskit is launched into the air as a huge Swampert bursts out from beneath the swamp. It catches Surskit in it’s hand and looks at you menacingly. . It then fires a Mud Shot right at you! How will you react?
