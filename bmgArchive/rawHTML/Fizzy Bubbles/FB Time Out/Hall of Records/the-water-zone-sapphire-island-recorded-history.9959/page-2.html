Sasuke looked at his choices. He and Ino had turned right after arriving on the beach, when most of the people had set off in the opposite direction. Now they were all but alone, and had reached a lush, green part of the island, where they had a number of options to chose from. A sparkling lake lay ahead, and in the distance a gushing waterfall spewed from the rocks. A turquoise river flowed rapidly toward the jungle, and to the right was a murky jade swamp. ”Right Ino, which way?” his Whimur bounced up and down, straining to get a better viewpoint. Sasuke bent down and picked her up, perching her precariously on his shoulder. She wrapped her long soft ears round his head for extra stability and looked out over the surroundings. “Mur!” she squarked happily, indicating the jade swamp, much to the disappointment of Sasuke - the other options seemed far more appealing. The hot swamp was covered in a foul looking vapour and the muddy water (if you could call it that) bubbled menacingly. ”You sure you want to go there? I’ll have to carry you all the way,” he coaxed in an attempt to persuade her to change her mind, but Ino was having none of it. She crossed her ears across her chest definatively, ”WHIS. MUR.”
With a reluctant sigh Sasuke walked off toward the Jade Swamp with Ino purring happily on his shoulder.
