Emerald Forest Please reply in Limegreen

Shinjara

Sakura bops along behind you as you enter the jungle. Bugs buzz, birds chirp, very jungly sounds that are normally heard in a jungle echo around and around. Once or twice, you even hear a monkey hooting off in the distance. But what's odd is there's no Pokemon. Not a single one. Sighing, after hours of walking around, you sit down on a tree trunk, the humidity getting to you. Sakura wraps her vine around a tree branch, pulling herself up and hanging loosely from it, closing her eyes.

You don't know how you lost track of time, or when you zoned out, but a small voice brings you back to reality. "Excuse me...miss?" You shake your head and see a man standing in front of you. He has a safari outfit on, right down to the sand-coloured khakis and wearing socks with sandals. "I couldn't help but notice you and your Victreebel, and well...I was wondering...would you like to battle? My research gets very boring very quickly, and I don't want to harm any of the wild creatures around here." He reasons. "If you have no objections, shall we make it two on two?" He then inquires, looking hopefully at you.

What do you respond?
