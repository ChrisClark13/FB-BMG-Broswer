Welcome to the Staff Prize Shop!​

When you're ready to reap the benefits of the time you've spent collecting your hard earned points, the process is pretty simple.
- Simply link to your withdrawal from the Point Reserve when purchasing items.
- Await confirmation before using said items. Told ya it's easy! Mwahaha!


Prizes:

Miscellaneous
5 Points: Move Tutor Pass
5 Points: Double Daycare Pass
10 Points: Double Move Tutor Pass
25 Points: Custom Hold Items (Devil Horns, Bunny Ears, Lucky Rabbit's Foot)
25 Points: Custom Effect Items (Ruby Pendant, Cupid's Bow & Arrow)
35 Points: Custom TMs (Glass Rose teaches Thorns; Magical Snowflake teaches Ice Barrier; Chocolate Carrot teaches Chocolate Magic)
2,000 Points: Devolution Spray
5,000 Points: Shiny Spray

Egg House
200 Points: Egg House Pass - Guaranteed Rare Egg
500 Points: Egg House Pass - Guaranteed Super Rare Egg
750 Points: Egg House Pass - Starter Egg
1,500 Points: Egg House Pass - Ultra Rare Enigma Egg

TCG
55 Points: Energy Booster Pack Pass
75 Points: Booster Pack 1 Pass
90 Points: Booster Pack 2 Pass
115 Points: Booster Pack 3 Pass
135 Points: Booster Pack 4 Pass
200 Points: Theme Deck Pass

Bases
250 Points: Set of 5 Base Tokens
400 Points: Set of 10 Base Tokens


Please note
- Only a maximum of five of each kind of pass can be purchased at any one time (ie, 5 Move Tutor + 5 Daycare, but not 10 Move Tutor or 10 Daycare).
- All passes must be used immediately after purchase.
- All passes, including TCG passes, are not tradeable.​

Subject to change...
