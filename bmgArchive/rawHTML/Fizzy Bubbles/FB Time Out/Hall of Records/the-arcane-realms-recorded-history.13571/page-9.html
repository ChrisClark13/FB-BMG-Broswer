Slick Rick: Though you're well aware of the danger involved with staying here any longer, especially considering what could very well be on its way right now, there is no way you can deny Sandshrew its request. Glancing nervously across the desert you take the time to kneel down and smile at the little sand mouse, agreeing to grant Sandslash the dignity of a burial as a sign of respect not only for the fight she'd put up to protect her offspring, but to help Sandshrew to move on with the peace of mind coming from knowing that its mother has been properly put to rest. Lifting Sandslash from the ground, you gently place her into the hole Sandshrew has dug before filling it in. Staryu, touched by the moment, plays its part by spinning on the spot while spouting water into the air. In the desert heat, the water evaporates before it even hits the ground, but the misty spray leaves behind a beautiful little rainbow which hangs in the air just above Sandslash's grave. While you stand and watch, a shiver runs up your spine as you feel Sandshrew's little paw in your hand. Closing your fingers around it as a sign of consolation, you're surprised to see Sandshrew sitting silently by the grave with tears in its eyes as it looks up at the rainbow Staryu has made to signify that Sandslash will always be watching over her baby. So if it's not Sandshrew beside you, then what...?

Looking down you find nothing is there but upon opening your hand you find a Soft Sand laying in your palm. Though this is a strange occurrence indeed, you have no doubt about the fact that while Sandslash may be gone in body, her spirit is lingering on and that she's grateful to you for having helped her baby. Turning your attention back to Sandshrew you offer it some kind words, assuring it that its mother had loved it very much and that you're happy it had found you in its time of need. Removing an empty Pokéball from your pocket you hold it out to the little sand mouse, offering it a home. Sandshrew's teary little eyes look between your face and the Pokéball several times before it smiles and nods. Turning back to its mothers grave, Sandshrew gently touches its paw against the tiny mound, saying goodbye to the parent who had sacrificed her own life to protect it before turning back to you and willingly triggering the release mechanism on your Pokéball. Sandshrew is drawn inside by the red capture beam, and now that you know the Lv.4 Sandshrew is safe and sound inside of your Pokéball you know it's time to get out of here, and as you're preparing to head off you hear a hair raising roar in the distance which you know is your cue to hurry along and leave now. Casting one final glance toward the rainbow covered grave, a light breeze passes over the sand causing it to shift and move into a pattern. Taking a closer look, the etched image in the sand creates the sign of Sandslash's paw pointing in the opposite direction to which you were about to head. Even in death, Sandslash will always be watching over her baby...
