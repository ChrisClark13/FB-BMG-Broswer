To become a member of Fizzy Bubbles you will first need to make a Registration Post providing certain details. These details include your Home Board, your name, your Starter Pokémon (chosen according to the Rules), it's level and gender, along with any miscellaneous details you wish to provide about yourself and/or your Pokémon:


* Remember to include your Home Board in the "Title" box
Code: 
[noparse][size=1][b]Name:[/b] 
[b]Starter Pokémon:[/b] 
[b]Miscellaneous Info:[/b] [/size][/noparse]
You can add extras if you wish, such as age, gender, personality, appearance, background, etc., but these details are not required.

An example would be like this:
Title Box: Bulbagarden
Reply Box:
Name: Arnie
Starter Pokemon: Lotad (Bob), Male, Lv.5
Misc Info: Lotad and I like to travel, making as many friends as possible.

After you have done this a Mod will check to ensure you've posted correctly and send you a Registration Test. At this point your signup post cannot be edited, even if it's only to fix a minor typo. Upon passing the test you will be notified via PM, after which you may post in the Starter Pack Thread picking up one pack of your choice. Your next step will be to join the FB Usergroup by going to your User CP > Group Memberships > Fizzy Bubbles. We will keep an eye out for your application and approve you so that you will be able to re-post your details as an official Members Post that you can edit with any details omitted from your original application. Once this is complete you are good to go and will be able to begin adventuring in the zones. Be sure to update your Member Profile regularly to add any Pokémon and/or Items you obtain during your adventures. Most importantly, check and recheck all of the shop and zone rules BEFORE posting to ensure you are doing so properly. While the Registration Test will cover most of the rules, it's still always best to double-check before posting.

Please DO NOT post signatures when you apply. If you do post a signature or post your details incorrectly, you will be skipped until your application post has been corrected.

If you do not reply to the Registration Test within one month of it being sent to you, your registration post will be removed from the thread and you will need to reapply if you still wish to join. Some people apply but never respond so we will delete inactive posts in order to avoid clutter.

You cannot sign up with a new username if you are a current FB member or have at any time in the past had an FB account. This is considered a "Double", or "Secondary" Account, and will be banned if we discover the deception. If you are an inactive member, PM us and we will return your details to the main FB forum. Full details are in the Rules Thread so please read them.

The Bulbagarden Registration Thread is for Bulbagarden members so you must have been a member of this forum for at least three months prior to applying to FB. Once registered you cannot change home boards without a valid reason (such as you being banned from the forum so are unable to post in FB on your own home board). If signing up during a promotion, a home board change is completely off the cards so please be sure this is where you want to be before making your application post.
