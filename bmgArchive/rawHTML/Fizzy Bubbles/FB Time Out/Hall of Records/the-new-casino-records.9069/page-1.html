Bi-monthly Track Racing:
Bet on a random number of Rapidash of many different colors.
Each ticket is 2 Coins and there are a variety of bets you can choose from and you can purchase as many tickets as you like. However, you can place no more than 3 on any one type of bet!! Races will happen about every other week. Win, place, and show odds for the next race will be posted at the end of the previous race.

Odds for all Rapidash will range from 1-1 to 20-1. Accordingly, place and show odds will be better than win odds.

NEW
You can bet the following ways:
Win: Pick the horse you think will win. (Win)
Place: Pick the horse you think will at least place. (Win or Place)
Show: Pick the horse you think will at least show. (Win, Place, or Show)
Quinella: Pick the first two horses to finish in any order. (Win-Place)
Exacta: Pick the first two horses to finish in exact order. (Win-Place)
Trifecta: Pick the first three horses to finish in exact order. (Win-Place-Show)
