Welcome to the Cable Club.
Link up and trade Pokémon or Items with your friends. Pokémon may be traded between two players to evolve them.

Step 1:
- Post a Reply with the Pokémon you want to trade.
- Make sure you have all evolution items or requirements mentioned as well.
- You can also trade Items with fellow trainers.

Step 2:
- When someone comes along they may reply agreeing on the trade.
- Trades may be permanent or traded back after an evolution or other arrangements.
*** Make all arrangements ahead of time to avoid conflict.***

Rules:
- You must have at least 1 Pokémon or Item up for trade if you post in here.
- You may post a want/have list containing 4 Pokémon or 4 Items that you want/have.
* You must have something that you can trade in return.
- You may post a new trade after your last one has been completed.
- Do NOT Spam, make all trade arrangements via Private Message, AIM, YIM, MSN, or Etc.
* Do NOT ask or offer in this thread.
- Other than Rare Candy you may NOT trade anything you can obtain in the Pokémart.
- The max amount of Rare Candy you can trade for is 4.
* You can combine 4 Rare Candy with other Items or Pokémon in the same trade.
- Clearly state what you are trading and to who.
* Always "Quote" the person you are trading with.
- Post *Trade Confirmed* including what you are trading and to who.
- Post *Trade Closed* when your trade is complete.
- There should only be 3 posts made for making a trade.
* The Offer, the Confirmation, and the Closing of the trade.
- Once your trade iscomplete delete your haves/wants list and re-post a new updated version.
- Do NOT delete your trading posts.
- Pokémon up for trade take a spot on your team.
- DO NOT post your signature.
- You CANNOT trade your starter Pokémon.
- The Cable Club works on a first come first serve basis.
- The Cable Club is not an auction.
- Do NOT involve yourselves in other peoples trading.
- You CANNOT give away or trade your Pokémon and Items if you are leaving Fizzy Bubbles.
* When you leave your account is destroyed.
- Failure to follow rules will result in deletion of posts and a possible banning from this shop.

Here is a sample of how a proper trade should look: Char416 has noticed that Pikaman has a Jigglypuff he wants to trade from looking at his Trade List. She wants his cute little Jigglypuff and has a Pikachu which Pikaman wants badly. So she used the forums Private Messaging System to offer a trade and PMed Pikaman. Pikaman and Char416 worked out a trade over Private Messages and agreed to trade.

Since Char416 wanted to make a trade with Pikaman and they both agreed to the trade she posted in the Cable Club with a "Trade Offer." She clearly states all the details of what she is trading, for what she is trading for, and to who she is trading too. Pikaman responds to the offer and posts a "Trade Confirmation." He makes sure to quote Char416 also posting a *Trade Confirmed* and clearly states what he is trading, what he will get in return, and to who he is trading too. Char416 then posts a "Trade Closing." She quotes Pikaman's trade confirmation with a *Trade Closed* stating in her post what has been traded, what she got in return, and who she traded with.



Char416 posts:
Trading Lv. 5 Male Pikachu to Pikaman for his Lv. 7 Female Jigglypuff.






Pikaman posts:
Char416 said:
Trading Lv. 5 Male Pikachu to Pikaman for his Lv. 7 Female Jigglypuff.
Click to expand...
Trading Lv. 7 Female Jigglypuff to Char416 for her Lv. 5 Male Pikachu.
*Trade Confirmed*





Char416 posts:
Pikaman said:
Trading Lv. 7 Female Jigglypuff to Char416 for her Lv. 5 Male Pikachu.
*Trade Confirmed*
Click to expand...
Traded Lv. 5 Male Pikachu to Pikaman for his Lv. 7 Female Jigglypuff.
*Trade Closed*




Prior trade arrangements made, trade offer made, trade offer accepted and confirmed, and finally the trade has been closed.
