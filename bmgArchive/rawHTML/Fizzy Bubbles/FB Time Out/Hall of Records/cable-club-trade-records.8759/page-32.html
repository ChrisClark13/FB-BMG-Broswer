While others that were older and more mature, took to more serious activities, leaving poor Leo in the middle, but with the Shinx...they were the same.

Liza bent down to stop their game of wrestling as she grabbed each of them by the paw, both panting but smiling. “From now on, you two are brothers, you will look out for one another.”

It was barely mid day, when Liza read her messages and she found out that a Pokémon she was after since the beginning of her journey would finally be hers.

So far the game was tied, but she noticed that her new electric type was a natural at being able to spot exactly where her Growlithe was hiding, much faster then Leo could sniff out where his younger brother was.

Not wanting to disturb their play, Liza found a near by terminal to use, while watching her Pokémon play from the corner of her eye.

Swiping her card across the designated slot, Liza plugged in the amount she promised and sent it over right away, waiting patiently for the machine to spit back a nice new Pokeball in return, containing a brand new Pokémon to her team.

She grabbed the capsule and held it for a moment, thinking back, how much her family pestered her to get a water starter as her first Pokémon. They just couldn’t understand that fate had intervened then, and that Sam her Charmander was a gift from the heavens...

She didn’t want to do the usual introduction here though and opted to look for a better location, while calling to her two Pokémon, the lass trainer and they hurried right outside as she noticed the same open lake that she had first introduced herself to her now Milotic.
