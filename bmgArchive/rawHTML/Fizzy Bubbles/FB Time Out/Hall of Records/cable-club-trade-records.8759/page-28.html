On a dark stormy night a young women was laying in bed flipping through the channels of her hotel’s complementary cable TV. She stopped when she came across an old TV show she had watched as a child, “I Love Lucy”. She soon found herself having an odd feeling of nostalgia fill her stomach and started remembering who used to be by her side watching along.

Sighing, she thought about calling him, or sending him a message, but…it wasn’t that easy.

She startled when she heard a knock come from her door.

Rushing over in her PJ’s, she opened the door to find…nothing. She looked both ways and no one, no one was there, finally she was about to close the door when she looked back and saw a box with a purple bow tied around it seating ontop of the mat.

She grabbed it before closing the door behind her. She sat down looking at the box for a while before deciding to open it. Inside was a lonely red and white ball with a note attached that read: “Thought of you.” -Love Ethan.

A warm smile crept on her face as she hugged the ball.

Quickly standing she threw the ball in the air yelling “Come on out!” before a candle like creature with a bright purple flame came into view.

“Awwww, aren’t you just the cutest!” the young women squealed! Causing the candle Pokemon to hid behind a desk chair.

“Ohh, I didn’t mean to frighten you” she said in a low voice, “I’m Liza, and I’m sure he wouldn’t have given you to me if he didn’t I think I could take care of you.”

Slowly the Litwick moved in closer, “Trust me, I don’t bite,” shaking her head, “Why don’t you and I watch something?,” she asked as the Litick nodded in agreement.

“I’m sure if you spent a while with him he made you watch some old TV Shows, this one here is one of our favorites” she said as she lifted the Litwick into her arms, who gave a little giggle.

As they sat down to watch the show a thought passed through her head.

“Hey, I know, I’ll name you Luz, short for Lucy, would you like that?”

“Lit!”
