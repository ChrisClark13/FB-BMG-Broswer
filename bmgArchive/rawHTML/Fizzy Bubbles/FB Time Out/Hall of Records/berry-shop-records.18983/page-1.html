Welcome to the Berry Shop where you can find the finest berries in all of Fizzy Bubbles! From the most common berries to the rarest of the rare, we have them all! Attach them to your Pokémon or visit the Pokéblock Shop and use one of their wonderful Berry Blending Machines to make a delicious treat for your Pokémon, the choice is yours!

Every Wednesday we will give away one free random berry and all you need to do is visit to collect it. Make sure you check with us each and every day however, because at random times we will give away other berries and there’s no telling what time or day or even for how long these giveaways will last. Sometimes we may only have one or two, at other times we may a whole sack of them and sometimes if berries aren’t picked up on time they will spoil so you will need to be quick if you want them.

Rules
- You may only pick up the berry given away by the Shop Owner or its employees (All FB Mods), during the timeframe specified.
- Please do not post for any berry of your choice. All berries given away will be named by the employee giving them out.
- When posting please state the name of the berry you are picking up (for linking purposes). Any berries not named will not be accepted when you need to use them.
- You may only pick up random, special offer berries for yourself, not for other members unless you have been picking up for someone on a regular, long-term basis. Weekly berries are the exception - you may pick up for somebody else if they have asked you to do so because they will not be here on a Wednesday to pick up for themselves.
- All links to all berries must be kept until the time comes for them to be used. Even if you trade a berry, the link must be used in the Cable Club so the Pokéblock Shop Owner can see where the berry originated from.
