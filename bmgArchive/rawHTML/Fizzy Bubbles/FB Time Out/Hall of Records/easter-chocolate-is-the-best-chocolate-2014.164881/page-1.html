Chocolate Bomb (Normal)
Producing a Chocolate heart in front of them, the user proceeds to throw the Heart at their opponent which on contact explodes donig Damage equal to Egg Bomb, when the heart shatters 3-5 little pieces of chocolate appear and can be eaten by anyone including the opponent, when eaten the chocolate heals a small amount of HP equal to what a Sitrus Berry would heal.
Contest Type:Cute
Contest Effect:If Last to appeal uses a Cute Move, this move gives them + 2 appeal points and you +3 Appeal Points (+ regular appeal points)

Re: Easter Chocolate is the Best Chocolate...

Happy Fizzby Chocolate Bunny Day!!
(Otherwise known as Easter)

I've gone a full 6 weeks without chocolate and these goodies I've got for you are so mouth watering ... you better get in fast before I eat them myself =p
Here are some great little chocolate treats to help your Pokemon learn something new:

1 x Golden Egg (Cashable for 3,000 coins or can be used as a pretty base decoration)
1 x Chocolate Milkshake When eaten by a Pokemon it allows them to learn a new EM or MT move they have yet to learn. (Non-Tradeable). Exp: 1st May 2014.
1 x
Chocolate Memory Berry which, when eaten by your Pokémon, will allow it to pluck information from its own DNA to help it relearn four moves
it has forgotten through evolution, or to learn four egg moves it wasn't born with. In some instances, your Pokémon may even learn a combination of
Relearned and Egg Moves. (Non-Tradeable). Exp: 1st May 2014.

Go mad and pork out, fill your faces with chocolate and Enjoy ^-^​
