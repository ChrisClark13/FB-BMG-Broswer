"...still wanna know who the hell throws little golden egg statues at parade floats," Jack grumbled. Disguised as his Trainer once more, the Ditto rubbed his borrowed head in a spot that roughly coincided with where most of the Shiny Golden Egg Statuettes had landed on him.

"Hey, they could've done worse," Keith pointed out, grinning as he heard the clattering and clanking within his backpack, coming from all that gold. "Besides, you're fine. I've seen you take a Bullet Punch while Transformed into an Avalugg, you tell me how this is worse."

"Well, for one thing, I'm pretty sure that between the two, Avalugg has a way higher Defense stat than your average parade float," said Jack. "But yeah, all things considered, the crowd could've done worse," he conceded. "You gonna sell those?"

"Naturally," Keith nodded. "But yeah- these Rare Candies and Marshmallows, though," he added, holding up a nearly-empty marshmallow bag. which he was currently repurposing to hold all the edible goodies he was walking away from the event with. "Some seriously good stuff. And these Marshmallows, I think they got some actual benefits if eaten by Pokémon, too. Even got a couple of Rindo Berries in the mix."

"Well, I fer one am tired out," yawned Meowth, still sitting on Keith's shoulder. "Wat say we head on home?"

"Sure," Keith smirked. "Just as soon as you say it."

"Eh?" Meowth replied. Then, he sighed. "Oy... youse was right about da marshmallows, OK?"

Keith smiled and nodded. "Damn straight I was. C'mon, let's go home," he added, leading the way back to Sludge Wave Coast.
