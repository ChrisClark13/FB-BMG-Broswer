Gary could hardly believe that it had been a full year since he returned to the region of Fizzytopia. So many memories had happened over that time, and so many new friends and teammates had been made. Much like the festivities that had drawn him back to the region a year ago, the people seemed to be in high spirits once again under the pretense of a new tradition. Eager to check out the festival for himself, Gary headed down the Fizzytopian streets with his trusted Typhlosion as hit side and a squishy, round blob in his arms.


Holding Pickle up so she could see everything that was going on, the shiny Pyukumuku silently took in all the sights and sounds. This time of the year marked the anniversary of when Gary hatched the sea cucumber from her egg, so Gary felt it was fitting to partake in the celebration with her. A low grumble came from within Pickle and Gary chuckled. The Pyukumuku was mostly stomach, so he wasn’t surprised that she was feeling hungry. “Alright guys, let’s get something to eat,” he announced. Typhu licked his lips and followed, not protesting in the slightest.


The trainer and his two Pokemon enjoyed a quiet picnic in the park where they devoured a tasty meal. For dessert, Gary served Pickle a sweet Pecha Cobbler, which she inhaled with delight. “Pyu!” Pickle exclaimed, going up to kiss Gary on the cheek as a token of appreciation. There was an odd feeling at first, as if some of his energy had been drained away, but Gary shrugged it off and finished up his meal. As they rejoined the festival, Gary and his Pokemon enjoyed a concert that was played by a rock group. Once the show was over, he was handed a few promotional CDs and stowed them away. It was getting late, so it was time to go home. Pickle had long since fallen asleep in Gary’s arms, so Typhu and he decided it was best to depart, hoping to come again next year.

"Psst. Wake up, Pip. We're here."

A sleeping penguin gradually stirred in the girl's arms, blinking blearily at her bright and bustling surroundings. Colorful balloons and blaring brass music boldly filled the air, though the familiar sounds of laughter and celebration only caused the baby bird to shy a little, shrinking into her trainer's shelter. Beside her, an Azumarill glanced worriedly up at Jess as she tried her best to calm the small Piplup.

"Hey, it's okay. Nothing bad will happen this time, I promise."

"Zu!"

Atzu piped up in reassurance, vowing he would protect them if necessary. Pippa smiled a bit and relaxed, agreeing to give it another try. It had been a long time since Jess had last been out with the pair, and their last excursion had unfortunately ended in disaster. She was determined to make today a success though in order to make up for it.

"Come on, guys. Let's have some fun."

They wandered through the stalls, eagerly checking out all the games and merchandise, which included statuettes of the founders and collectable Lapras plushies of the region's mascot, Nessie. ...When Jess read the name, for some reason she felt a pang in her chest again, but pushed it aside. Instead she lifted Pippa up onto a giant plush's back as Atzu bounced up to join, posing for a group picture.

Soon it was time for the main attraction, as the party made their way towards High Street to watch the Great Fizzytopian Parade go by. While Pippa was clearly nervous at first given how the last show of floats she saw went up in flames, she quickly became distracted by the shining festoons, eyes asparkle as she couldn't help but grow caught up in the festive spirit.

A particularly archaic-looking part of the procession passed by, seemingly sponsored by local museum. Apparently they were advertising a number of artifacts recovered from the Ruins of Alph recently, as they handed out free souvenirs to the spectators (which Atzu again used his high jumping abilities to catch on Pip's behalf). Again, a sense of nostalgic regret filled Jess as she guiltily made a mental note to make up for many of Gumpy's explorations being cut short as well.

As the day drew to a close, the gang gathered by the bonfire to unwind and enjoy some warm cocoa. A volunteer handed them a bag of goodies containing berries and marshmallows, which gave Jess an idea as she suggested to Atzu to use his CHOCOLATE BOMB to create some chunks of sweetness. She then pulled out a box of graham crackers and showed her friends how to roast marshmallows to make s'mores, which they devoured with delight. As fireworks began to light up the sky, Jess felt her own heart bursting with joy, glad to be together with her two precious teammates - and that for once nothing catastrophic had happened to interrupt their perfect day.
