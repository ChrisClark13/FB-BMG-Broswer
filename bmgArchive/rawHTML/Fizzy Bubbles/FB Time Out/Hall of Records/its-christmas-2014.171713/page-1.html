Wes took aim and lobbed the sprig of mistletoe into the frigid southern sea they’d been voyaging across over the holidays, the icy surf gobbling it up as it quickly lost altitude. Reaver and Toxique were sparring above a deep sea trench, the smaller Skrelp taking Camouflage in a field of seaweed. The Kingdra was good at sniffing her out by now, veering in her general direction at tenacious speed… Suddenly! The sprig of mistletoe caught him on the snout, Toxique seizing the chance to break her cover and give the seahorse a kiss…

~Toxique goes from Lv.46 to Lv.48. Reaver is already Lv.100

~Toxique (Modest) goes from a Beauty Stat of 0 to 20. Reaver (Rash) already has a Beauty Stat of 100

Reaver looked violated as the mock kelp Pokémon hooked her tail around his, flashing a brilliant white as she began to metamorphosis. The sea dragon’s spine twisted and elongated, her pectoral fins growing like weedy branches and her tail coiling further around Reaver until he was completely smothered. The light ran off her murky figure, revealing a brilliant red-green headdress. Reaver was looking lightheaded, although that may have been courtesy of the sea dragon's Poison Touch…

Toxique evolved into
