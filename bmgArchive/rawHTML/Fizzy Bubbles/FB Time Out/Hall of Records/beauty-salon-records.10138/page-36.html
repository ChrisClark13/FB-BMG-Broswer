There's no confusion here, hon Margaux resides in a Luxury Ball which gives two BP's. You register those points here as you did with your initial dropoff telling me she has no BP's and is in a Luxury Ball. Beauty points gained from special balls can only be claimed once, not each time your Pokémon stays. So that's two BP's from the Luxury Ball, plus the two she gained from staying for one week (2+2=4). You then dropped her off for another week where she gained an additional two BP's (4+2=6) (again keeping in mind her LB points have already been claimed so you can't claim them again). Today you'll be picking her up from yet another week's stay, so 6+2=8. Margaux has eight beauty points as of today.
