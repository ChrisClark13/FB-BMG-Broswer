Pumpkin Pack containing:

x1 Rare Bone

x1 TM Energy Ball

x3 Gold Gummi's

x1 Psychic Ball
x1 TM Psychic
x1 Holographic Dark Alakazam Trading Card

Vampire Pack containing:
x1 Super Pass
x3 Pink Gummi's
x1 Halloween candy

x1 TM Toxic
x1 Holographic Dark Gyarados Trading Card

Spectral Pack containing:
x1 Super Pass

x1 Halloween candy
x1 Pumpkin Memory Berry
x1 Rare Bone
x1 Ghost Ball
x1 TM Shadow Ball
x1 Holographic Dark Dragonite Trading Card

Zombie Pack containing:
x1 Super Pass
x3 Black Gummi's
x1 Halloween candy
x1 Pumpkin Memory Berry
x1 Rare Bone
x1 Mummy Ball
x1 TM Snarl
x1 Holographic Dark Blastoise Trading Card

Notes:
* The Halloween Candy gives 2 levels to Dark and Ghost Type Pokemon, but one level to any other Pokémon. Sableye and Spiritomb, both being Dark/Ghost Dual types, will receive FOUR levels. If banked, the Halloween candy is the equivalent of a regular rare candy.
* When eaten by your Pokémon, the Pumpkin Memory Berry will allow it to pluck information from its own DNA to help it relearn four moves it has forgotten through evolution, or to learn four egg moves it wasn't born with. In some instances, your Pokémon may even learn a combination of Relearned and Egg Moves.

Please accept a select few of the following spooky items:-

Ghost Ball: Increases capture rate of Ghost Pokémon
Rocket Ball: Increases capture rate of Dark & Poison Pokémon
Mummy Ball: Increases capture rate if your Pokémon has a Contest Ribbon
Wereball: Increases capture rate of Canine Pokémon
Franken Ball: Increases capture rate of Steel Pokémon
Pumpkin Ball: Increases capture rate of Grass Pokémon
Vampire Ball: Increases capture rate of Pokémon with fangs


You may also choose ONE of the following plushies:

*
Shedinja Plushie (w/Silver Powder) – when hugged one Pokemon gains 2 levels
*
Absol Pluhie (w/Blackglasses) – when hugged one Pokemon gains 2 levels

*
Misdreavus Plushie (w/Spell Tag) – when hugged one Pokemon gains 2 levels
*
Gardevoir Plushie (w/Twisted Spoon) – when hugged one Pokemon gains 2 levels
