The door to the Trainer registry burst open. A young girl in a blue dress, exhausted from what would seem to be a long bike ride, stood in the entrance, catching her breath. She'd been very excited for this day. Finally, she would be able to begin her adventure in Fizzytopia. The registry closest to her old house had been closed for...who knows how long. In fact, all of them had been. It was only recently that this one had opened. Heather's mother had been hesitant, but at last, she'd been allowed to apply, and passed!

"I'm here to register!" She called, walking up to the counter.

"Heather, is it? Yes, your approval has been processed. Here is your starter kit!" The woman at the counter handed Heather a yellow box. Inside were several items.

"A Pokedex, a pack, a berry Pouch...PokeBlock case...Fast Ball...Rare Candy...Aaand a Magnet! And...some spending money...and coins! Oh man, we're set to go here! Thank you, miss!" Heather said to the worker, packing some of the items away in her new pack. She pulled a PokeBall from her waist, and released her partner.

"Hey, Grace! It's official now!"

"Really? You got it? Awesome!" Grace chirped, as Heather scooped her onto her shoulder.

"Come on, now. The world awaits!" Heather walked out of the office, ready to take on the world...
