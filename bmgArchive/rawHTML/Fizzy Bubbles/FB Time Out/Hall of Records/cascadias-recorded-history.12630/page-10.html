Wayne – Ion Island Reply in yellow Repost
Your Houndour shakes its fur off to get rid of any water from the last attack. The Crocronaw opens up its claws and electric tendrils fly through the air towards Dante. Dante opens his mouth and Swift stars hit the Crocronaw. Fortunately its concentration was broken and the Thunder wave was unable to hit Dante. The crocodilian pokemon races over to Dante in a full body tackle motion. Just as it reaches Dante the Crocronaws body glows an intense yellow and its body is covered in electric energy. The Spark attack hits and causes Dante to fly back a bit and he retaliates with a Smog attack as the Crocronaw once again approaches but with a Thunderpunch ready. The Smog cloud engulfs Crocronaw and it tries to strike Dante, but misses. With fire energy in his mouth Dante releases a Fire Spin tornado, which surrounds Crocronaw and the Smog. Just as it seems things are going your way suddenly the Smog and Fire Spin seem to be lifted off the ground. You see the Crocronaw looking up with its jaws wide open and you see that it is using a Whirlpool attack to lift the attacks. The Fire Spin and Smog slowly dissipate due to the Water.

“Great job Crocronaw! Now get in there and Bite down on it!”
Crocronaw opens its massive jaws up and runs towards Dante, but it is suddenly shocked by an attack from the sky. You and the other trainer look up to look for the source of the attack. You see a horizontally floating Magneton with a man in camouflage seemingly floating between the three combined Magnemites. The other trainer sneers at the man.

“Well if it isn’t our hometown hero Lt. Surge! What, you realized Vermilion City wasn’t as great as you thought!”

Hearing the name Lt. Surge makes you think for awhile, realizing that it sounds familiar. You finally remember that there is a gym leader by the exact same name at Vermilioin City. He too used Electric types and of course as a gym leader is considered an elite pokemon trainer. You wonder what he is doing here when he should be back at Vermilion City taking gym challenges. The Magneton suddenly floats rapidly down towards the ground and Lt. Surge says, “Magneton let’s go! Thunderbolt then Tri-Attack on that Crocronaw.” Seems like he’s attacking the other trainer.

What will you do?

Jonouchi - Elegy Fields Reply in orange
You accept Mr. Manager’s invitation to see this peculiar item that he found and you follow him and Cade to wherever Mr. Manager left it. As you follow them Mr. Manager and Cade talk about the progress of the building up of the circus. After walking through probably half of the circus you arrive at what used to be a clearing. What now sits in the clearing is a huge RV van/trailer. Mr. Manager opens up the door and Cade and yourself follow him inside. The inside of the RV is crisp clean and outfitted with what seems to be the most advanced technology for such a vehicle. There are buttons everywhere for who knows what and the furniture and home system seem to be luxurious. Mr. Manager and Cade keep walking and you follow them to a little room. You all sit down as Mr. Manager grabs a bag. From inside the bag he pulls out what looks to be a drum or two connected together. The shape is like an hourglass, with two triangles pointing at each other.

“It’s beautiful,” Cade remarks as he gently grabs the drum from Mr. Manager.
“Yes, I must say it was quite a find by Donny.”
“Heh, Donny. His ability to find things is amazing.”
“When he sensed it he just bounded off and I had to run after him. I say we found it around 50 feet from the outer edges of the circus. So what can you tell me about it Cade.”
“Well it looks very familiar and this is no ordinary cloth that makes up the drum. It is silky smooth and I can only surmise one Pokemon’s fur that it could be.”
Cade hands over the drum to you and you feel the silkiness that Cade describes. You wonder if this is the treasure that the Vulpix is talking about.
“The fur can only be of a Ninetail’s and this would correspond with a wives tale that I heard when I was a young boy. It went like this… A pair of Ninetail’s had an offspring that was inquisitive and smart. For reasons unknown the Ninetails had to sacrifice themselves in order to save their child. They promised to protect him and so lent their furs to produce a drum that the child could play with and call upon their spirits for comfort.”
“An amazing tale indeed Cade,” Mr. Manager says as he takes the drum from you. “I wonder what mysteries this drum holds…” He then pounds the drum lightly making a “Pong” sound.

Moments later the door of the RV is pounded and Mr. Manager opens the door asking what the matter is. A frantic girls in a panic says, “Mr. Manager there seems to be hundreds of Slugmas coming this way!” All of your eyes widen at the sound of the imminent danger. Mr. Manager grabs a pokeball from his belt and then hands over the drum to you. “Ricco I must ask you to keep this drum safe from any damage. I have a feeling this drum is special. Go back to the large circus tent and protect that as well. The circus depends on you!” You all head out of the RV as Mr. Manager and Cade head out to face off against the hoard of Slugma.

What will you do?

Lee-san - Salin City Reply in silver
You have full confidence in Guts and his ability to battle and so you give him his orders. Guts sees Surskit skate off and away from him, but Guts immediately rolls up into a ball and races off after him in a Roll Out attack. Surskit is easily faster than Guts and has a couple of feet of breathing room as the cat and mouse game begins. Surskit easily traverses around and over cracks with grace and style as it releases a Sweet Scent around the field. But, as the chase lasts longer and longer it is apparent that Guts is catching up to Surskit. Surskit gives a glancing glare back at Guts as he is about to hit it and jumps up above an elevated crack in the ground. Guts jumps up after it and in mid-air Surskit turns around to face Guts and charges an Ice Beam. Guts instead of going straightforward for Surskit he suddenly drops down to the ground and slams the ground with his entire body. The force of the impact sends chunks of rocks and rubble flying straight up from the ground and it hits Surskit who is still falling from the jump. Surskit caught off guard prematurely launches Ice Beam and it hits the ceiling of the building. Surskit lands atop of Guts and Guts takes this opportunity to throw Surskit off of him and the bug plops on the ground a couple of feet away. Both pokemon have used up a bunch of energy this round, Guts more than Surskit. But, Surskit got a big chunk of HP taken away from Stone Edge as well being thrown by Guts.

What will you do?

Jerichi – The Red Forest Reply in green
You get up in a hurry in order to help Officer Jenny. You pick out a Moon Ball from your belt and release the pokemon held within it. Your large aquatic weasel, Floatzel aka Demyx, emerges and looks at the scenario in front of it. You give orders to Demyx and he bounds over next to Hitmonlee and fires a Swift attack at the wild woman. Officer Jenny yells over to Hitmonlee, “Lee let’s fire along with this trainer’s Floatzel and use a Hyper Beam.” Hitmonlee nods and begins charging up energy in front of it. Demyx’s Swift attack soars past the wild woman and leaves some scratches on her slowing down the charge of the dark energy. Demyx and Hitmonlee fire off Water Gun and Hyper Beam respectively and the wild woman fires off the massive dark ball. The different energies clash and it is unknown how much longer Hitmonlee and Demyx can last against the ball. Suddenly a large blast is heard and the ball erupts and everyone is sent flying from the force.

You open your eyes as you lay face first on the ground and realize that a tree stopped you from flying even farther. Officer Jenny lies next to you and she soon stirs and you both see your pokemon. Hitmonlee lies atop Demyx, but soon moves. Unfortunately it is only Demyx who rises as it seems Hitmonlee took the brunt of the force as he stood between Demyx and the blast. Demyx unhappy with this spots the wild woman still in the trees, but in some of the lowest branches. He charges the base of the tree and runs up the tree with all his momentum and bites down hard on the wild woman’s ankle with an Ice Fang. The wild woman screams and shoots black lightning bolts from her eyes to make Demyx let go. Demyx falls back to the ground and glares back up at the woman. The wild woman’s eyes then begin to glow red and she looks down upon the area and the place is soon lit by the same eerie red as the woman’s eyes. Officer Jenny tries to walk backwards, but it seems her attempts are futile, as she can’t escape the area for help. Demyx just growls as he makes an attempt to jump and bite the wild woman.

What will you do?

Stacey turned to face the voices pulling her black hood over her face, her eyes underneath set in an angry scowl, all to be seen of her face was the skull on her hood its empy eyes seemingly as angry as hers. Casting a quick glance to the cave Stacey noted that they couldn't possibly retreat back there, the soldiers were surely filling it to capicity; she could only hope there was a cave in they all suffered a slow suffocating death.

Two soldiers had made out their positions and with their equally fierce looking pokemon were bearing down upon them. A dragon pokemon Stacey vaguley recognised as a Garbite and the familar Mightyena tore towards them ready to do to them what they had done to the helpless villagers ... only they weren't helpless, far from it.

"We stand and fight" Ranjona exclaimed boldly and stacey couldn't agree more. She cupped her hands to her mouth and gave a short whistle. From the sky a large balloon-like pokemon hovered down ... a Driftblim. She had yet to see one of these amazing creastures and allowed herself a moments leave whilst she admired it, she only hoped to obtain one herself one day.

Brought sharply back to reality by the barking of the ever nearing dark pokemon. Lowering her head her fingers brushed over a Ghost Ball on her belt grasping it and pulling it from its magnetised lock. Enlarging it and allowing the purple mists of flicker over her fingertips she looked behind her and gave a short order to Dru.

"Watch that cave, let nothing come out of it. You spot anything untoward then let your Supersonic fill the caverns."

Dru hummed and clicked as she danced back towards the cave. Stacey turned to the face the soldiers, a smirk hidden by her hood creeping over lips as she released the pokemon within the Ghost Ball. A small flash illuminated the darkness before all was left in its place was a hovering purple creature, its disembodied hands curling and flexing drawing patterns in the air. It glanced back to its trainer its dark eyes hard and jagged teeth brought together in a sneer.

"Narcissa, time to bring some pain," Stacey said simply eliciting a small smile from her Haunter, having since her evolution, relaxed a little around her mistress.

"Driftblim is with us, take out those pathetic dark creatures advancing us. Lets take Mightyena shall we. Mean Look, show it that we do not fear it. Then let it burn with Will-o-Wisp and finally strike with Shadow Punch."
