You agree to help to Volbeat find his lover before sundown, prompting a strange happy-sounding noise from the firefly. You look up in the sky to see how much time you have. You make up your mind and tell the captain that you wish to serve the crew rather than leave on a raft. Several cries of “Yo-ho!” arise from the crew. After mopping the deck, you are allowed to retire to your quarters, a small corner of the cargo hold. You have a moment of peace before a large crash breaks the calm. You and the Aipom quickly head outside to investigate. The entire crew, even the captain, is outside with weapons surrounding a Gyrados. A small section of the boat was missing and the Gyrados had fresh blood on its teeth. You also notice that a sailor is missing from the group. The Gyrados uses Ice Beam and freezes the entire crew and half the ship. You and the Aipom are the only ones left unfrozen. Your Pokéballs are all still intact also.

What will you do?
