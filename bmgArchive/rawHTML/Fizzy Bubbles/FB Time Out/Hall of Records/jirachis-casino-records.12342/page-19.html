Jirachi's Casino

Welcome to Jirachi's Casino! Just as Jirachi is said to grant your wishes, Jirachi's Casino hopes to make your greatest wish for unimaginable wealth and riches come true! We’re now offering a larger variety of ways in which to make or break your coin purse but everyone wants to win so what are you waiting for? Get out your dusty old coins and start gambling to help make your greatest dreams come true!

Rules
1) When posting in the Casino you must post a link to your current coin balance from the Coin Exchange.
2) Read the instructions for each of the Casino Games very carefully before posing to ensure you are wording your bets in a way which is easily understandable. Casino staff are not responsible for errors made due to misinterpretation.
3) All wins and losses must be recorded in the Coin Exchange so your coin balances can be maintained as accurately as possible.
4) Have fun gambling!


Gambling Machines

Starter Slots
Each reel has one of each Starter Pokémon: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle, Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Totodile, Treecko, Torchic, Mudkip and Pikachu!

No Matches/Combos: 0 Coins
Two of a Kind: Get two of the same Pokémon and win x2 coins you've deposited.
Three of a Kind: Get three of the same Pokémon and win x10 coins you've deposited.
Starter Combo: Get all of either RB or G/S/C or Ru/Sa to win x3 coins you've deposited.
Same Type Combo: Get three of the same type and win +50 coins you've deposited.
Two of a Kind + Same Type Combo: Get two of the same Pokémon + another of the same type and win x2 coins deposited +50 more coins.
Pika Pika Combo: Get all three Pikachu and win x10 coins you've deposited + 50 more coins.

Minimum Bet = 10 Coins.
Three RNG rolls will determine the results.
You may roll up to 5 times per day.

Roulette
The Roulette Wheel has six colors and four Pokémon.

Colors: Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Violet
Pokemon: Pichu, Wynaut, Azurill, Togepi
24 Total: Red Pichu, Orange Pichu, Yellow Pichu, etc., etc.
Matching Color = x5 coins of your bet.
Matching Pokémon = x3 coins of your bet.
Matching Specific Color and Pokemon = x15 coins of your bet.

Minimum Bet = 20 coins per spin.
Maximum Bet = 500 coins per spin.
You may place only one bet per spin
You may spin up to 5 times per day.
Two RNG rolls will determine the results of each spin.


Lotteries

Daily Pick
We have 40 tickets available per day. Half of them give no prizes whatsoever, but the other half have prizes of varying rarity attached to them. Some are great while others are average. Would you like to try your luck? If so, simply post selecting a number between 1 and 40 (if somebody has already chosen the number you would like then you’ll need to choose another), then wait for the Daily Pick Lottery to be updated. Fixed Daily Pick tickets are also available, so if you wish to reserve a specific number to avoid somebody else choosing it before you can post, you must pay up front to reserve your ticket in advance for up to a minimum of 20 draws or a maximum of 100 draws.

You may only choose one ticket per day.
Cost = 40 coins to play.

Weekly Fantasy Five
Pick 5 Numbers between 1 and 40 and hope to win. Numbers do not repeat.
Match 2 Numbers = 20 Coins
Match 3 Numbers = 100 Coins
Match 4 Numbers = 400 Coins
Match 5 Numbers = 2,000 Coins

Cost : 20 Coins per ticket.

Monthly Stockpile
Each month you may choose 6 numbers between 1 and 20. Numbers do not repeat. You need to get at least 3 numbers right in order to earn a prize but if there is no winner, there will be a rollover to next month's competition. In the Stockpile, the money won for matching numbers is based on how many tickets were purchased so come on people, what are you waiting for? Get your numbers in!

Current Jackpot = 11,470 coins

Match 4 Numbers = ?? Coins

Races

Bi-Monthly Horse Racing
Welcome to the Fizzy Bubbles Race Track where, twice each month, eight of our finest equines go head to head in some of the most exciting horse races FB has ever seen! There are a total of eight Rapidash, each one a different color. In order to play, the payment for each ticket is 20 coins and there are a variety of bets you can choose from. You can purchase as many tickets as you like however, you can place no more than 3 on any one type of bet. Races will happen about every other week. Win, place, and show odds for the next race will be posted at the end of the previous race.

Odds for all Rapidash will range from 1-1 to 20-1. Accordingly, place and show odds will be better than win odds.

You can bet in the following ways:
Win: Pick the horse you think will win. (Win)
Place: Pick the horse you think will at least place. (Win or Place)
Show: Pick the horse you think will at least show. (Win, Place, or Show)
Quinella: Pick the first two horses to finish in any order. (Win-Place)
Exacta: Pick the first two horses to finish in exact order. (Win-Place)
Trifecta: Pick the first three horses to finish in exact order. (Win-Place-Show)


Mystery Cruise
Every three months a limited number of tickets are available for trainers to undertake the cruise of a lifetime. When the SS Anne leaves the Slateport Docks, you'll want to be on it so in order to gain a place on the ship you'll need to try your luck in the Casino. While travelling the high seas there is no telling what will happen. You may make new friends or perhaps meet a rival or two, but whatever the case this is something you don't want to miss out on. Each week one ticket will be given out. Simply post asking for an SS Anne Ticket along with handing over a payment of 20 coins, and you will be allocated a number. At the end of the week, one RNG roll will determine the lucky winner and your name will be added to the Passenger List for the next departure.



Now start gambling for those precious coins and earn those fabulous prizes!
