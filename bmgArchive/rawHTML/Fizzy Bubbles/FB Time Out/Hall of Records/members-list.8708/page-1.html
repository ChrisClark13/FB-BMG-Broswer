Gary's Past:​
Well, it's not much of about Typhu's past, but this is more of Gary's past. Gary's career as a Pokemon trainer started not when he was ten, like most other trainers start off, but when he was the mere age of eight. See, Gary grew up in a small town in the great land of Jhoto, called Azalea Town. Having his cousin Bugsy inherit the Pokemon Gym at a very young age himself, Gary was constantly overshadowed by his prodigy of a cousin. However, they got along like best friends, and Bugsy being his elder, always watched over him and let Gary play with all of the gym pokemon. Even though Gary seemed to have a bright future with pokemon, he never really saw them as much of anything other than toys.

One day, terror struck the great land of Jhoto, the tiny town of Azalea in particular. Yes, Team Rocket, who had been thought to be defeated some three years before, had arrived on Azalea's doorstep, in a bit of a pinch for an economic rebound. Their for of doing so was by taking Azalea's trademark pokemon, the Slowpoke, and cut their tails off as a delecacy. As disgusting and inhumane as it sounded, they sold for quite a profit, and the villans seemed to have a pretty profitable situation on their hands. They had quickly overpowered Bugsy and all of the very few trainers of the town, such as Kurt, the pokeball craftsman.

Being the naive, curious kid he was, Gary tried sneaking up to the Rockets' temporary base of oporations in the Slowpoke Well, bringing his pet Slowpoke, Pokey, along. However, he accidentally tripped and was about to be beaten by a Team Rocket's Koffing, when this mysterious trainer showed up with his Croconaw, and easily defeated each and every Team Rocket member there. Seeing that Croconaw battle so fiercely, that event had become a turning point in Gary's life. Never before had he seen such heart and soul put into raising a pokemon, and what it took to overcome the toughest opponents. Gary was awestruck, deciding that from that day forth, he too could become such a strong pokemon trainer and defend his town if needed.

Though he never got the chance to thank the mysterious trainer or even say goodbye, Gary will always remember what happened that day, and went to the gym every day with Pokey. There Bugsy taught him the basics, but Gary too was finally blooming as a pokemon trainer, thus making Bugsy's training sessions only slightly beneficial.

~~
Two years had passed, and Gary had shown a lot of growth as a pokemon trainer. It was then time that he did start his very own pokemon adventure, but he made one major decision. He did not leave with Pokey, who was now a Slowking, but instead, he left his beloved pokemon in Azalea to defend all of the other Slowpoke in the town park, where they all lived. So Gary then left his homeland behind, with a Love Ball as a parting gift that was made by Kurt, and went on the adruous hike to New Bark Town, where the laboratory of Professor Elm was located. At the laboratory, he was joined up by two other youngsters, and the three starter pokemon were presented to them. He gazed at the Chikorita, Cyndaquil, and Totodile in amazement, remembering how the mysterious trainer had used a Croconaw, the evolution of Totodile. Gary then also recalled his dreams of striving to become the best trainer there is, and the only way you can do that is by overcoming tougher opponents. Looking down at the small, blue and tan, fire echidna, Gary smiled. He picked up the Cyndaquil, and from that moment on, they were fast friends. He named the little fire critter Typhu, in honor that he had the spirit of a Typhlosion, packed into his tiny little body. The other two trainers chose their pokemon as well, and the trainers all challenged each other to battles. However, Gary clearly had more experience than the other two combined, and together he and Typhu walked over them in battle. Gary then knew of the Pokemon Gyms, and decided that he and Typhu would defeat these places and make their way to the pokemon league.

On their way to Violet City where the closest Gym was located, Gary and Typhu defeated countless trainers, and his ego soared, along with their abilities. On a rainy, windy night, the night before he was to arrive in Violet City, Gary encountered a person he thought he'd never see again. It was in fact, the mysterious trainer who had rescued the whole of Azalea Town all those couple of years before. Having won so many battles, Gary foolishly thought he and Typhu were ready for anything, and challenged him to a battle. The trainer flatly refused, not willing to take on a trainer weaker than him, but Gary persisted, and continued to do so, until he had foolishly angered the trainer, who finally accepted. The trainer sent out a Feraligatr, and the battle had only begun, when Typhu was brutally crushed into critical condition, and the trainer left, dissapearing from Gary's life once again. This was yet another turning point in Gary's life, as he kneeled down in the mud, holding his greatly injured Cyndaquil. He vowed to never abuse the power of a pokemon's strength, and never again would he push his pokemon to such a feat that was dangerous to their health. Dazed and confused, Gary wandered through the forests of Jhoto, as he tried to nurse Typhu back to health. It was a few weeks or so before Typhu was able to do any battling, and until then, Gary had to fend off the wild Hoothoots with sticks. After about a month of travelling alone with Typhu, the two stumbled into the great port of Olivine City, having taken a direct path across all of Jhoto, missing each and every town that was along the coast. Since they were way too weak to try taking on the Gym leader there, who had a massive Steelix, they heard rumors of a brand new region. Gary and Typhu worked and worked, battle after battle, to earn enough money to take a ocean-going ship to this new place. And so, on their first anniversary of battling together, Gary and Typhu finally boarded the boat to start their adventures once again.

Unfortunately, a storm had erupted in the middle of the voyage, and the pair became shipwrecked, floating to a strange new set of islands, inhabited by a few civilized locals. Going from island to island, four more years had passed of Gary and Typhu travelling together, until they finally reached the land of Fizzy Bubbles. Overjoyed, the now fifteen year old and his pokemon began their journey anew...
