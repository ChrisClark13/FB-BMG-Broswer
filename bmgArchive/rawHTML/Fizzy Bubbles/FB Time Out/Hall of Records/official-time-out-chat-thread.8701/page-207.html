So here I am thinking I'm never going to find a chicken burger I like. Me, the girl who loves chicken and burgers with equal enthusiasm. I've tried all sorts of chicken burgers from goodness knows how many places who my friends have sworn, "make the best chicken burgers ever!" Yes, well. If those things were tasty then I'd rather eat my shoes.

Then I got taken out to this little nondescript place for a mothers day dinner last night - a place that sells American style food. By God the menu was impressive, but I was told that I absolutely MUST have a burger. My eyes kept going to the Southern Fried Chicken Burger. The description looked good, but I knew from experience that chicken burgers have always let me down. However I decided to take the plunge....

And wow! You Americans have been holding out on me! I finished it and wanted more. I didn't of course, choosing to order a smashed pavlova sundae for dessert instead. Now I'm eager to go back and get myself another chicken burger because I've finally found one I like! Can't wait!
