From a battling(trainer battle) perspective, what "role" does each of your Pokemon do(feel free not to list them all, I'm not gonna because of time restraints):

Gemini (Weezing): They tend to go for more long-range, projectile moves, like Sludge, Psybeam, Thunderbolt, Flamethrower, Blizzard, Shock Wave, things of that sort.

James (Vileplume): All-out attacker, generally, but will use status moves when necessary. Usually Sleep Powder to gain a head start on the attacking.

Nagini (Arbok): You'll see what her battle style is like once her battle against Orchrist is picked up.

Hebenon (Qwilfish): Jack-of-all-trades. And I do mean ALL trades.

Hedwig (Crobat): Mixed attacker. Favors physical moves like Crossfire and Cross Poison, but will not turn down a chance to show off the fact that she knows Thunder.

Lily (Venomoth): Like James, she cripples the opponent with status before going into all-out attack mode.

Sirius (Mightyena): Sets up with Howl, then sweeps with physical moves. If Poison Fang manages to poison, he'll go for Venoshock.

Malfoy (Toxicroak): Mixed attacker, though leans heavily on the physical side of things. In a double battle, he can also heal up his teammate with Heal Pulse.

Goyle (Victreebel): I've yet to RP him using any attacks, period, let alone figure out a battle style for him.

Marvolo (Seviper): Mixed attacker. Prefers to immobilize and subdue a foe with Wrap whenever possible.

Pomona (Ivysaur): Annoyer. Greatly prefers status moves, though does pack some select attack moves for when the time is right. Notably takes advantage of moves that capitalize on her cuteness (Charm, Captivate, and Attract), and also makes use of the PoisonPowder/Venoshock combination.

Peeves (Dusclops): Leaning more and more towards physical attacker, a nod to his previous life as a Machop. Favors punching moves in particular.

Salazar (Scolipede): No preferred battle style yet, he basically just does what works best for each situation.

Crabbe (Gulpin): No preferred battle style yet, he'd just cripple opponents with Yawn, then launch his attacks.

Scorpius (Skorupi): Mixed attacker, though leans heavily on the physical side. Packs Flamethrower as a trump card, and prefers to use Night Slash to try and take advantage of Sniper.

Myrtle (Shuppet): All-out attacker. She's not particularly one for status moves. Then again, she doesn't know many status moves at the moment.

Luna (Swoobat): No real preferred battle style, though she is fond of helping out allies in double battles with her Helping Hand move.

Strychnine (Grimer): Attacking tank.

Meowth: No actual battle style yet.

Bellatrix (Skuntank): Haven't worked out a battle style for her just yet.

Aragog (Ariados): Prefers to subdue opponents first, with moves like String Shot, then goes all-out with the attacking.

Severus (Dustox): No preferred battle style.

Hermione (Weedle): To make up for her horribly limited movepool, she improvises wherever possible. Particularly fond of combining moves in clever ways. IE: Gummi Bomb, followed by Electroweb to make the Gummi Bomb harder to remove.

Six (Foongus): Prefers to make use of move combinations, such as Defense Curl/Rollout and PoisonPowder/Venoshock.

Pisces (Blue-Striped Basculin): All-out attacker.

Helena (Shuppet): Haven't worked out a battle style for her yet.

Rubeus (Stunfisk): Once he learns more moves I'll think of something.

Millicent (Timburr): Same as Rubeus.

Cyanide (Umbreon): Annoyer- fond of status moves, particularly Toxic.

Ginny (Pawniard): Physical attacker.

Chuck (Gastly): Haven't worked out a battle style for him yet.

Helga (Makuhita): She does whatever works in a given situation.

Melittin (Beedrill): Tank. Uses various attacking moves, but defends himself with stuff like Harden and Reflect.

Hannah (Qwilfish): I'll work on that once she's got more moves to work with.

Chance (Wartortle): Mixed attacker, though leaning heavily towards the special side of things.

Vernon (Tauros): Will be a mixed attacker, once he gets more moves.

Fang (Carnivine): Will work that out once he gets more moves.
