Jesus, am I that obvious? And yes, that is the plan. We've been mulling it over for a while as it will give us the power to do what needs to be done without having to run to Admin all the time. The new one will be FB's 4th branch and quite possibly a new "Home Branch" seeing as it'll be easier if we can deal with everything immediately although we'll still have to go to Admin at our other three branches. I've already set up a free site with a free server just so I can mess around with Admin controls, style sheets, and figure out everything that needs to be done so I don't mess up a "paid" board and I've found that it's actually a lot simpler than I had originally thought. I don't plan on keeping the current one though seeing as it's only for practice. I want a vBulletin Board and I'm perfectly willing to pay for a decent server. This new site is also not specifically Pokémon related though it will have one section of the forum totally devoted to Pokémon. I don't want it to be a copy of other sites, I prefer to be original. I'm already working up a logo for the new site and messing around with Photoshop to hopefully learn how to create decent graphics. Although we have Ichi for graphics he can't always be around and I don't want to be bugging him all the time when we want or need something done.

It's not going to be happening anytime soon though. We still have three sites that need a lot of work before they'll be "self-sufficient" so to speak, but we really do need to be able to have more control over our own members. FB has grown so large that it's responsible for over half of UPN's total posts and even at Serebii it takes up a large chunk of the boards (post related and including the FB Club), especially when you take into consideration that it hasn't even been running for a year compared to some of the other forums on the board. Bulbagarden is still pretty quiet but that's only because it didn't have a large community to begin with but it will grow over time and once we have other zones under control and I can hire more ZA's over here we'll have a new zone that ALL FB members (UPN and Serebii included) can post in.

The problem is that with so many members we have the odd ones who feel it's their duty to cause trouble and when that happens we're powerless to do anything about it. We have to go to Admin and sometimes (depending upon which board is in question), it can take a while for anything to be done about it and in the meantime our hands are tied. It's frustrating. If FB were smaller it wouldn't matter so much but that's not the case. We're a huge community and we have a lot of members/threads/posts to watch over yet we're treated like lepers compared to other mods who do a fraction of the work and are given more privileges. The only way we can take any control over it is to make our own site.

However, as I previously pointed out, it won't be happening anytime soon. When I do something I like to know it's being done properly and there's no way we can deal with a 4th branch before our other three are completely under control. Even when the site is eventually created it won't become public knowledge until it's completely ready for posting and that includes all forums (including FB) being ready to go, rules, smilies, avatars in place, style sheets created... A lot of things need to be taken into consideration so don't be expecting to see it pop up in the near future cuz it ain't gonna happen.
