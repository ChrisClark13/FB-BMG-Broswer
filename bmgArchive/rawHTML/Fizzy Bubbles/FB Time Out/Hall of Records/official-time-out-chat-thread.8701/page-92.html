Arn gave me the best example, I think. He told me to consider a battle between a Snorlax and a Caterpie. Snorlax isn't really going to lose, unless the trainer starts making it do stupid things, or uses up all its energy. The Caterpie obviously won't win, unless the trainer uses some damn clever tactics and has Caterpie go "all out" and consume energy at a much faster rate.

That's why I like incorporating more RP into battles. Sure, you can tell me what moves you do, but you can do -so- much more with your moves here than in the games. I remember your Totodile using Water Gun to propel itself into the air, you can do that in these Trainer Battles! I mean, I decide what -happens- when you do that, but isn't that more fun than shouting "Use Water Gun!" over and over?
