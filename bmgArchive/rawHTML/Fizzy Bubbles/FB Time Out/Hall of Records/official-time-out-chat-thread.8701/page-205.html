From a battling(trainer battle) perspective, what "role" does each of your Pokemon do(feel free not to list them all, I'm not gonna because of time restraints):

Most of my team is built around speed and high attack/special attack power. It's not really balanced as well as a well rounded team should be, but it fits my style quite well. Here are how my Level 100 pokemon break down.

Typhlosion: He is pretty much a fast specialty sweeper. His Fire type attacks are his bread and butter. He does have more than a few physical attacks like Earthquake, Double Kick, and Dynamic Punch. The custom FB moves he has are one of the few things that set him apart from an average Typhlosion.

Dakota, my Spearow: Flyer who is a physical attacker. Personality-wise he is definitely a head on attacker. He does have a few trick moves like Faint Attack and Aerial Ace to throw off opponents, but basically, he just goes right at opponents.

Lucy, my Houndour: Lucy is my condition applier who uses annoyance moves like Toxic, Will-o-wisp, Taunt, Embargo, and Spite to put opponents in tough spots before hitting them with stronger moves like Shadow Ball, Fire Blast or Crunch.

Tarka, my Floatzel: Tarka is the epitome of my hit them fast and hard style of battling. His already high speed is increased after using Rain Dance and he hits hard with moves like Surf, Ice Beam, and Crunch.

Arielle, my Luxray: Arielle is a little more of a hit them hard than hit them fast. She still has good speed, but seems a little slow compared to some of the other members of my team. Her power in both attack and special attack compliment her movesets well with Thunderbolt, Fire Fang, Ice Fang, and Night Slash working with both her attack and special attack stats.

Grovyle: Much like the rest of my team Grovyle is a fast and hard hitter. I have recently been working on adding more status affecting moves, but for the most part he goes with Leaf Blade, Energy Ball, and Dragonbreath to beatdown opponents.

Gar, my Blaziken: Gar is my fighting specialist with a little extra firepower. Most of his moves are physical with a touch of extra in them. The Elemental punches, and many of the standard fighting attacks are his main focus. Earthquake and Aerial Ace give him a little extra coverage, but for the most part he is a bruiser.

The rest of my team are still growing into their roles and like these will continue to develop over time.
