Pomona, Dennis: Not too far from where a certain Turtwig and Bulbasaur were, another pair of Grass-types were taking a walk through the park. However, while Zyra was clearly in love with Toby, these Grass-types were more in the way of siblings. She then offered one to the Bulbasaur, but he had to grab it in his mouth. Pomona, noticing this, stopped so the two of them could eat where they were. Once they had eaten their Pecha Berries, they started walking again, only now Dennis looked a little downcast.

"Pomona, why can't I grab stuff with vines like you can?" he asked sadly.

"Why, what'd you do?" Dennis asked curiously.

"I sneaked up behind Keith and tickled him like crazy!" giggled Pomona. "He couldn't stop laughing, he's extremely ticklish."

"That won't make him mad, will it?" the Bulbasaur asked nervously.

Pomona laughed. "Of course not!" she smiled. "You shouldn't worry so much, Dennis, Keith's a great Trainer."

"I just hope it's not a problem that all I can use right now is Tackle," the Bulbasaur murmured.

"Don't worry about that," replied the Ivysaur. "Nitro only knows Charge and Flash right now, and Cadmium's only attack is Vice Grip. When Keith first hatched me, I only knew Tackle, too, but Keith wasn't bothered by that. The first thing he did with me was teach me how to use Sludge, in fact."

"Oh, yeah, you got some good attacks, right?" Dennis asked, now smiling again.

"Yep!" Pomona replied proudly. "Ooh, ooh, check this out, in fact, tell me what you think," she added, backing up a bit. "Ever since Keith asked me if I wanted to enter Pokémon Cute Contests with him, I've been working on possible performances for the appeal round. Check this out." At this, Pomona jumped up into the air, started spinning, and pulled her body into a tight Defense Curl, leaving only her flower protruding. And then, still in the air from that jump, her flower shot out a Petal Dance, encasing the Ivysaur in a sphere of swirling petals. A pair of purple glowing vines protruded from this, whirling around just as rapidly as Pomona was spinning- she was using Power Whip, her vines acting as helicopter blades to keep her suspended in midair as her flower added more and more petals to the sphere. Finally, the vines retracted back into the sphere, and it started falling. But then, before it hit the ground, Pomona unfurled her body from the Defense Curl, causing the swirling petals to disperse in every direction. To top it all off, she managed to even throw in a Charm attack for maximum cuteness as she unfurled her body. She landed on the ground, the petals gently falling to the ground all around her.

Dennis was in awe. He got up on his hind legs, leaving his front legs free to applaud enthusiastically. "Wow!!" he exclaimed. "That was amazing! Did Keith see that yet? You were awesome!!!"

Pomona smiled modestly. "Heh, thanks, Dennis," she grinned. "I'm still working on it before I show it to Keith, though. "What about you? You think you might want to enter Pokémon Contests, too?"

"I dunno," the Bulbasaur replied. "I'm actually more interested in battling, to be honest." His eyes lit up for a few moments as he pictured himself as a powerful Venusaur, toppling his opponents with incredibly strong attacks. Then, he glanced nervously at his older sister. "...Hey, Pomona," he added, "It's fine that I want to become a Venusaur, right? I mean, I know how you feel about that..."

In response, Pomona extended her vines and gave the young Bulbasaur a reassuring hug. "It's perfectly fine," she confirmed. "Just because I don't want to evolve into a Venusaur doesn't mean you shouldn't. Your evolution is your decision." Once the hug stopped, she grinned at Dennis. "Hey," she added, "wanna race to the Watmel fields over that way?" she asked, pointing down the path with a vine.

"You're on!" Dennis grinned. And with that, the Bulbasaur and Ivysaur took off, dashing madly towards the grove of Watmel Berries.
