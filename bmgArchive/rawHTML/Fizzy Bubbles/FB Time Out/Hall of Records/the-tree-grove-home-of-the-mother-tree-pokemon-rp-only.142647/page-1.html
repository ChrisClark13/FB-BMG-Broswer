In days past, the Tree Grove was an incredible attraction for all manner of human visitors. In particular, a mysterious, gigantic tree known only as the "Mother Tree" once drew in researchers from across the land to the small grove in which it resides. The species of the tree, or indeed how it grew so large in the first place, has baffled scientists for centuries; even more baffling is the fact that the Pokemon who live in the giant tree appear to be smart enough to have formed their own little civilization, with a crude form of government, their own architecture and plenty of social interaction. Complex pathways and waterways at the base of the tree, structurally magnificent hollows within the tree trunk for hiding and relaxing and suspended tires and swings for play are all the handiwork of some particularly industrious Pokemon. These Pokemon fought particularly hard against researchers and trainers who were intent on studying/catching them, and in recent years, a ban has been placed on any humans from entering the grove in which the Mother Tree resides, since researchers, scientists and trainers were considered destructive to the unique natural habitat this wonderful tree provided for these Pokemon. Recently, however, the Fizzytopian government has reassessed their ban, and while they still do not want humans intervening in the Tree Grove, a statement has been made allowing trainers to drop off their Pokemon at the Tree Grove for fun and relaxation. It seems as if the Pokemon of the Mother Tree look forward to having some Pokemon-only company!

From the bustling treetops where flying-types build their nests to the deep network of underground tunnels that weave between the tree's mighty roots where ground Pokemon like to make their caves, to the inner hollow of the tree where dark and ghost types are rumored to play, there is something here for nearly every species of Pokemon. The Machamp at the entrance will be sure that your pesky trainer won't follow you in. Check in with the nice Furret at the counter, slither, scamper, fly or swim on in, and enjoy everything that the Tree Grove has to offer!
