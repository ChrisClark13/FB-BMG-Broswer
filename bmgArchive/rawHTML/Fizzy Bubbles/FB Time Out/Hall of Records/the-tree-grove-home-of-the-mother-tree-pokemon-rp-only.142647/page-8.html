OOC: Ordinarily I'd have waited for Dave to post so I could continue all three current storylines at the same time, but the idea for this came to me, and I started writing, and before you know it, I had this done, and I just couldn't resist, so here we are.

Captain Stomach, Rubeus, Vi, Conis, Brittany, Amelia: As the superhero Swalot struggled with the Ursaring, the four Pokémon continued on their way. "Heh, hear that?" sneered the Ursaring, in spite of his perpetually weakening state. "A weirdo, she called you."

"I heard, all right," Captain Stomach replied, his mustache continuing to drain Ursaring of energy. "And she also called you a clown." With that, the green tendrils of energy vanished, his mustache returning to normal as Ursaring collapsed in an unconscious heap. "And that's enough funny business out of you," the bold blob remarked. Then, he bounded away with swift swiftness, intent on making sure the Ursaring's underlings weren't trying to cause trouble on their own, sure though he was that he had scared them off.

Meanwhile, as the girls continued their trek, along with the berries they had gathered, no more vicious gangs of Pokémon barred their way. In fact, for a little while, they saw no other Pokémon, until they arrived at a clearing, peppered with nests for various species of Pokémon. It was a small village, though most of the activity seemed to be going on in the center. Vi and the others could spot a Stunfisk surrounded by numerous Pokémon, all of whom looked very young- Zigzagoon, Whismur, Sentret, Rattata, Diglett, and even a couple of Bunnelby could be seen at a glance. Oddly, the Stunfisk's fins appeared to be pure white, along with the exclamation mark that adorned his back.

The Stunfisk seemed to be focusing hard, and as they looked on, the exclamation mark on his back glowed with white light, and a glowing yellow egg materialized above it. The egg then floated forward, entering the body of what appeared to be a considerably injured Patrat. As the egg entered Patrat, the Soft-Boiled move did its work, and Patrat's injuries cleared up almost immediately. "Wow, thanks, mister!" the Patrat exclaimed happily. A couple of Watchog nearby, obviously this Patrat's parents, sighed in relief.

Just then, one of the Sentret who was standing on its tail seemed to notice the Combusken, Spheal, Mareep, and Taillow. "Hey! Hey, guys, look!" the Sentret exclaimed. "They got berries!"

As one, the group of Pokémon raced over, and before they knew it, Vi and the others were surrounded by a group of very young wild Pokémon, all of them looking up at them in obvious awe. "Wow, yeah, they got berries!" said a Zigzagoon excitedly. "I never seen so many at once! How'd ya do it?"

"How did you get past the Ursaring?" squeaked a very young Whismur. "They're really mean!"

One of the Bunnelby turned to face a nearby Diglett. "I bet that Combusken decked 'em all with Sky Uppercut or somethin'! Ursarings is weak to Fightin' types!" the Bunnelby exclaimed eagerly, excitedly making punching motions with his ears as he spoke.

As they continued to chatter excitedly, the Stunfisk flapped his side fins like wings and flew over to them. "My name is Rubeus," he introduced himself. "You see, this is Oran Village, named for the multitudes of berry trees not far from here, particularly Oran trees. I just found this place an hour ago myself, but from what I've heard, it seems that nobody in town can actually gather berries from the trees and bring them back to town anymore. Every time somebody tries, they're stopped by a gang of Ursaring who believe that the berry trees should belong to them and them alone. They eat from those trees constantly, and they show no mercy to anyone who takes berries from the trees. It's possible to maybe get away with a single berry, but even then, you'd have to be insanely lucky to achieve even that much without attracting their attention. This has been going on for almost a year now, apparently," he sighed, shaking his head. "I've been spending the past ten minutes healing everyone who tried to gather berries yesterday. Nobody's even dared to try today, thank Arceus."

"Yeah, Mr. Wubeus is weally nice," chimed in a baby Bidoof who couldn't have been much more than a week old. "He, he did that thing wiff the egg, you know, wike a Bwissey does, and, and he made me feew aww bettew."

"But how is it you all managed to get past them completely unscathed, and with so many berries?" the Stunfisk asked. "I mean, I would certainly believe the four of you to be capable of holding your own against them. You in particular don't look like someone to be messed with," he said, his latest sentence directed at the Combusken. "That said, though, the gang would still outnumber you... two to one, if what I hear is correct? If I didn't know any better, I'd say you got past the Ursaring without a fight at all... how did you get past the Ursaring?" he asked, sounding highly curious and vaguely impressed.
