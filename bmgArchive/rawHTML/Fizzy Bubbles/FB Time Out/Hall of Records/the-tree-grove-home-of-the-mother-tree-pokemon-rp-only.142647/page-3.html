August, Durandal, Seath - As the morning sun bursts through the branches of the Mother Tree, a shadow swoops over the Tree Grove, silent and graceful. A winged figure sails across the skies, surveying the beautiful scenery. At the request of her trainer, this powerful, yet gentle creature had traveled, by her lonesome, to the famous home of the Mother Tree. While Marion had not explained why the dragon's presence was required at such a place, August was not one to go against her trainer's wishes... After all, Marion always had August's best interests at heart, and the look in Marion's eyes, though decidedly worn out from a particularly exciting and sleepless night of partying, was full of excitement, with a hint of mischief (as always). It was strange that Marion had requested that August go alone, especially given how often Marion used August's trips as an opportunity to give one of her weaker Pokemon a bodyguard and travel companion, but again, it was not something that the loyal dragon-type questioned. Even so, in the flight over, August could not help but wonder what sort of plan had hatched in the ghostly maiden's mind.

Landing in the treetop with incredible precision and lightness for one so large, August bows respectfully to the various flying types roosted on nearby branches, who cannot help but bow back to such a regal Pokemon. Though August was not one to exert her power unnecessarily, it radiated from her very aura. She had been instructed to fly to the very top of the Mother Tree, where a surprise would be waiting for her. At the moment, there only appeared to be the usual scattering of nests and flying types, as well as a few interesting-looking treehouses built into the branches, though the Pokemon within were not of interest to the Dragon type. What on earth could she have been summoned here for? Ever the patient one, August perches herself on the highest branch, watching the skies for any sort of movement towards the Mother Tree. It was true that her surprise may not be coming from such a height, and yet considering the location of the surprise and the easy vantage point, August could not help but hope, with a flutter in her heart, that her surprise might be a pair of visitors so near and dear to her heart...
