At that instant, Helena made herself visible and went off in a totally different direction, one that would be clearly visible to the Corphish and Joltik. The idea was that they would mistake her for Myrtle and chase after her instead. There was only one problem. A Grimer who was bathing in the water nearby who had saw the Shuppet and put two and two together. He needed to alert Sparky and Bubbles. Sparky Bubbles there's trouble! Bubbles and Sparky turned around shocked to see there new teammate "running" towards them. Whwhat is it Botch? Asked Sparky. Still troubled about Botch being his teammate. I heard what you guys were saying and I want to help but I just saw another Shuppet listening to your conversation and it didn't look happy. Said Botch before continuing. I think that Shuppet is going to try and trick you! Just then the trio turned around to see two Shuppet moving in two different directions. Alright I'll take the one going to the right (Helena) you guys take the one on the left (Myrtle) said Botch. That does it said Bubbles getting even angrier then before. Alright lets go said Bubbles Oh and thanks Shrimp and Stench for helping. Said Bubbles. No problem. Said Sparky. Anything for love and justice! Said Botch before the group went there separate ways.

Well, it doesn't matter either way your stopping my friend Bubbles from confessing to Neville her true feelings. Now prepare for justice criminal! Yelled Botch before using harden and then used mud slap and aimed right at the Shuppet's face.
