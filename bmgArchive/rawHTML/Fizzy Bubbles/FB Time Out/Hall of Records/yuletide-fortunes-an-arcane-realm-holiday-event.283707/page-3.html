With the table of bags emptied, the ringing of a bell can be heard throughout the plaza. Everyone looks towards the tree, where the tolling of a crystal bell signals the beginning of the main event. Everyone lights their candle, to varying degrees of success, simultaneously. The lights in the plaza dim to the point where only the flames could be seen.
