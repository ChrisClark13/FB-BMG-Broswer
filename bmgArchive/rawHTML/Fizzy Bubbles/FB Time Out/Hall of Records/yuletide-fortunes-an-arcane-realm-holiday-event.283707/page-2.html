Yuletide Fortunes

An Arcane Realm Holiday Event!​


At the end of the year, the people of the Magician's Hamlet celebrate a holiday celebration that is well known throughout the Arcane Realm. Every resident of the Realm is well acquainted with this celebration, due to the various activities provided and the types of competitions that are shown off. Each day for twelve days there is a different competition that shows off a specific set of skills, all of which are done to decorate the grand plaza on the final night.

Of course, this year all of the contests have already been decided. The plaza is well decorated and lit up! The centerpiece of the plaza is a gigantic pine tree, grown and chosen by the keepers of the Forest of Wands. It's covered with various glass-blown ornaments, tinsel, and crystalized ice sculptures. Various benches made by the best carpenters throughout the realm, and a giant ice castle containing the tree was made by a mysterious ice sculptor. A grand feast lines the walls of the plaza, the culinarians for the realm bringing in local ingredients from each area to create an unforgettable feast.

This all is a prelude for the main event: The Vigil of Fate. A giant table sits before the ice castle, piled on with mountains of goody bags. For this year, due to the number of new residents in the Arcane Realm, there are five colors. A dark Royal Blue bag , a vibrant Sea Green Bag, a shimmering Golden Bag, a pastel Lavender Bag, and a beautiful Crimson bag. Attached to each bag is a blank card. Each participant is to grab a bag of their choosing when the time comes. They will be handed a candle to be lit by the spirit mediums and fortune-tellers of the realm. Everyone will light their candles at once, and then the "forces of fate" (Really just a powerful wind by the King's Royal Corviknight), will blow away the flames. The cards will reveal the person's fate for the next year, and a set of goodies will be inside to help them through the next year!

Now, trainers all across Fizzytopia are flocking around the square. From the beautiful tree to the promise of wonderful gifts, it truly is a magical time for the Arcane Realm. For the first time, these small holidays get to be enjoyed by all from around the region. Now come forth and make merry, and enjoy these wonderful gifts to help with your future endeavors trainers!

Trudge, trudge… with a weary head Emp could focus on little else but the rhythm of his footsteps against beaten snow. Naturally late to the yuletide festivities, the morning’s fresh powder had been reduced to dull slush underfoot. His threadbare sneakers offered no protection from the puddles of ice water, chills ebbing from his toes up to his spine with each unfortunate misstep. A wet foot was the least of his discomfort, hands calloused and forearms throbbing from weeks of hard labouring. It was a time of year for winding down – but not for Emp.

With a drawn out sigh he meandered through the swelling crowds; his waypoint was well marked, the alpine centrepiece dazzling as bright as the constellations it sat below. If that wasn’t enough to lift the workhorse’s weary spirits, perhaps the Royal Blue bag nestled at its feet would be.

Another year had come and gone, and it had been a busy one for Jess. Having broken out of her homeworm habit for once, she'd surprisingly traveled to a number of different places, meeting and making many friends along the way. Now, with the holiday season incoming, she felt like she could use a break from adventuring to just relax a bit and spend some time with her teammates, old and new alike.

With Christmas being considered a time for kinship after all, two separate "families" had tagged along with their trainer today: one phantom, the other furred. The Cakes were already well occupied with the feast spread out in the plaza, mother and father appreciating the fine tastes of good food, being accomplished chefs themselves. The two raised a toast to finally opening up their own home culinary business, and to many more years of success to come. Meanwhile their children stuffed their faces full before scampering off to play, soon engaging in a rowdy snowball fight in the center of the square; instigated of course by Pan almost immediately clocking his big sister with a wet white sphere, fully back to his mischievous self - only to be ganged back up on by both girls (although Mimi seemed more interested in glomping than actually participating in the mêlée, making love rather than war).

Watching the skirmish with excited delight, Yufi glanced to her parents, who nodded as she skipped joyfully to join the fray. The Furret and Linoone looked on with smiling pride as their daughter flopped into the fracas, before surreptitiously sneaking off to their own private corner beneath the brightly lit tree. Wrapping up together in a cozy bundle, like two long scarves sharing warmth, they sipped cups of steaming hot cocoa and nuzzled as they nestled... Only to be interrupted by a sprig of mistletoe strangely dangling down from branches above them. They glimpsed up to see their friend Minchi hanging from the boughs, grinning as she gave an eager wink and thumbs up. Kanna blushed furiously as she attempted to hide her face, but Kief gladly accepted the opportunity to gently lick her cheek - sending the shy Furret into a state of frozen shock, resembling a pink popsicle as Minchi quickly climbed down out of concern and waved her paw in front of her companion's glazed gaze.

Jess chuckled and shook her head as she quietly observed the scene, blowing on her own mug of piping hot chocolate as she admired the surrounding ice sculptures decorating the court, carved with such intricate detail and care. Though a part of her felt a sickened reminder at the sight, recalling a similar, far more gruesome image (a good thing Lilith wasn't here to witness either), their beauty was enough to repress the bile as she sat on one of the benches and simply unwound to the sound of festive merriment. A woman came round with a collection of candles, offering extras to tourists for the upcoming Yuletide tradition; however Jess politely declined, indicating towards the two candlesticks already pelting each other with a hail of pellets nearby, taking advantage of the cold castle ramparts for gaining high ground. The medium blinked in confusion, before shrugging and moving on (lest she herself become victim to a stray polar projectile).

As the time came for the main event, Jess clapped her hands and called the gang to her, as everyone instantly stopped what they were doing and approached the grand table, where a mountain of goodie bags awaited them. Kief and Yufi curiously sifted through the entire selection, sniffing and shaking each sack in an effort to determine what treasures were hidden inside, while Kanna tutted lightly but didn't make a move to stop them. Minchi, meanwhile, was more focused on choosing a hue that best matched her outfit, having dressed smartly for the occasion in a fancy, fashionable coat and fluffy handmuff. (The Cakes, on the other hand, appeared far less concerned about the color or worth than whether any sweets such as candies or cookies were contained within.)

Eventually the group settled on a lovely Lavender, as Jess picked up the pale pastel satchel and followed the crowd to the clearing in front of the castle, where the King's Royal Corviknight perched in anticipation of its duty. Lifting Patty and Pan in one palm each, Jess presented the two twins alongside the sea of soft golden glows illuminating the area, like fairy lights twinkling amongst the stars. Minchi helped elevate Mimi to get a better view as well, letting the couples snuggle as Yufi waved her Sneasel Wand along with the Court Magicians, summoning the "forces of fate" to blow through the tiny town, bringing in good tidings and cheer for the coming year...
