Mmmm .... Brains

I was working in the lab late one night
When my eyes beheld an eerie sight
My monster, from its slab, began to rise
And suddenly to my surprise

He did the Mash, he did the Monster Mash

Monsters have roamed Fizzy Bubbles through the Night and have left some interesting items behind. You can choose ONE costume for ONE Pokemon to be worn immediately in this thread, for they lose they're magic after Halloween.​

Werewolf Costume
~ When worn by your Pokemon they will be imbued with the fury of the mighty Werewolf and gain its strengths
*Raises 2 levels instantly
*Allows the Pokemon to draw on its instinct and learn 2 new egg moves
*Tough Contest Stats will raise by 20
~Once worn this Costume will revert into a hold item than can be held by any Pokemon:
*Werewolf Claws: This item raises the holder’s Attack strength by 20% but makes the holder 20% more likely to disobey orders


Vampire Costume
~When worn by your Pokemon they will be imbued with the viciousness of the cunning Vampire and gain its strengths
*Raises 2 levels instantly
*Allows the Pokemon to craftily learn 2 new MT moves without the aid of others
*Beauty Contest Stats will raise by 20
~Once worn this Costume will revert into a hold item that can be held by any Pokemon:
*Vampire Cape: This item raises the holders Sp. Attack strength by 20% but reduces its defences by 20%


Zombie Costume
~When worn by your Pokemon they will be imbued by the relentlessness of the un-natural Zombie and gain its strengths
*Raises 2 levels instantly
*Allows the Pokemon to absorb the mentality of one of their team-mates and learn two of their egg moves
*Cute Contest Stats will raise by 20
~Once worn this Costume will revert into a hold item that can be held by any Pokemon:
*Zombie Mask: This item raises the holders Sp.Defence by 20% but attacks with added effects inflicted on it are 20% more likely to be successful (on top of the existing percentages)


Mummy Costume
~When worn by your Pokemon they will be imbued by the haunting presence of the creepy Mummy and gain its strengths
*Raises 2 levels instantly
*Allows the Pokemon to learn 2 moves it wouldn’t ordinarily learn through level or TM: Miracle Eye and Psycho Shift
*Cool Contest Stats will raise by 20
~Once worn this Costume will revert into a hold item that can be held by any Pokemon
*Mummy Bandages: This item raises the holders Defences by 20% but reduces its speed by 20%


Scarecrow Costume
~When worn by your Pokemon they will be imbued by the deadly stealth of the eternal watchman, the Scarecrow and gain its strengths
*Raises 2 levels instantly
*Allows the Pokemon to stealthily obtain 2 TM’s without payment, to be taught immediately.
*Smart Contest Stats raise by 20
~Once worn this Costume will revert into a hold item that can be held by any Pokemon.
*Scarecrow Vest: This item raises the holders speed by 20% but due to the extra speed the Pokemon is more prone to misses and loses 20% of its accuracy with physical attacks
