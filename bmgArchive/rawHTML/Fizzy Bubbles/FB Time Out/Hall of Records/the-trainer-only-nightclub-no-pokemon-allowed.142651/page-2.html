Okiku walks into the nightclub, leaving the Pokéballs of her team to the bouncer at the entrance, as the idea of dropping them off at the nearby pokémon-only grove slipped her mind. Being not quite the social type, she felt a bit uncomfortable to be in such a social-biased place... but she overhead the place great taste in techno music (her favorite genre) but still open to other types of music, and the food is so great a Swalot has ruined the place countless times to the point he triggered the ban of Pokémon into the place. Being quite a fussy but passionate food lover and listening to her favorite music as a bonus, her curiosity won over her shyness to check it out.

As she looked around, she was surprised to find the place a bit quiet, with only a few patrons present. Okiku wasn't too sure what to think of it though, as less people meant less people to be around with, but it also meant to be somewhat forced to interact with them to bypass the more awkward situation of ignoring them.

To start things off though, she decided to sit at the bar and took a chance at ordering a Cake is Not a Lie drink (although she was tempted to get a a Dratini instead), and to her delight the barman was able to create it. She oversee her surroundings, shyly looking at the others, and secretly enjoying her drink, the music and the multitude of lights flashing, spinning and moving throughout the room.
