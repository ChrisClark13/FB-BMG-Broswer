Following not too far behind Connor was an eighteen year old, whose height betrayed her age: standing at only four foot eight, with a youthful face, it took an ID from her marble blue bag to convince the bouncers to let her pass. Alice Hope had heard that a club was reopening in central Fizzy Bubbles, and after her latest escapades, she needed to let loose.

The dress adorning her petite figure fitted the latest fashions, and clearly had taken some coins to purchase: the silk, satin and chiffron number was a mixture of hues, the blues, greens and whites meshing into many different floral patterns. It was darker across the bust and lighter at the hems of the skirt’s tiers, the forest green colliding with royal and tiffany blue on the lower half, which ended shortly above the knees. The trainer’s eyes resembled an anime character’s behind her new frames, which were far less scratched than the ones owned before.

It wasn’t too busy even though it was shortly before midnight, and the interior was interesting to be sure. The part she walked through, white spike heel pumps clicked on the floor, looked no different to a modern club. However, it appeared to have a pub inside it, too, where the booming music the DJ played failed to reach. Furthermore, there were no Pokemon in sight, and so she was glad that her team had been left behind at her flat to chill out (if her new Slowpoke allowed them to, that was).

“I’ll have a Pieces of Eight, please.” The teenager said, resting against the counter, dark blue gaze watching the bartender get to work. He fetched a long glass for the pale individual, mixing the chocolate, mint and alcohol together as the blonde admired herself in the mirror. Her nails were manicured and painted as always, long and glittering silver as if jewels had been encrusted on them.. Her hair was longer than ever, styled into a side parting with a wild fringe that touched her eyebrows. The rest was wavy, with some of her bangs falling beyond her shoulders, the rest left to cascade down her back.

Sliding the pay over to the bartender, Alice sat upon a stool and looked over the scene: a young man had entered just before her, one couple was dancing on the floor and a bunch of other patrons were gathered around a table. It was hardly the party rock she was used to, but starting out quiet could be nice. Hopefully, however, things would get more exciting soon enough...
